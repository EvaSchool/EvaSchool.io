EQUATION OF THE TANGENT LINE

The slope of the tangent to a curve at a point P is the limiting slope of the secant PQ as the point Q slides along the curve toward P. 

Determine the equation of tangent to a curve at a given point:

 

  1. Find the slope of the tangent 
  2. Use the slope and one of the points to form the equation of the tangent line.
