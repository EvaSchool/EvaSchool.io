Critical Points, Local Maxima and Local Minima

Overall Expectations

A1. Demonstrate an understanding of the rate of change by making connections between the average rate of change over an interval and the instantaneous rate of change at a point, using the slopes of secants and tangents and the concept of the limit.

B1. make connections, graphically and algebraically, between the key features of a function and its first and second derivatives and use the connections in curve sketching.

 

Specific Expectations

B1.5 sketch the graph of a polynomial function, given its equation, by using a variety of strategies to determine its key features, and verify using technology

Learning Goals

  • Students will know how to use the first derivative to find and test critical points of functions with the eventual goal of sketching function.
