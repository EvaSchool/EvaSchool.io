Learning Goals

Students will be review trigonometric ratios and the Sine and Cosine Laws.
Students can solve unknowns about acute and right triangles.
Students will be able to explain what vectors are and give examples of vectors in the real world.
Students will know how vectors are written algebraically and can use this information to add and subtract vectors.

Success Criteria

I CAN….
Explain the difference between a vector and scalar quantity.
Identify if vectors are equal or opposites.  
Add and subtract vectors.

Learning Goals

Students can multiply vectors by a  scalar and describe how this changes their appearance and properties.

Success Criteria

I CAN….
Recall and explain what is scalar quantity.
Explain Collinear and Unit Vectors.
Use the properties of Vectors in adding and multiplying vectors.

Learning Goals

Students are familiar with the “z” dimension.
Students know how to plot points in three dimensions.

Success Criteria

I CAN….
Recall and explain vector quantity.
Write the vector in component form for R2 and R3.
Write and plot the coordinates of the points in R.

Learning Goals

Students know what a unit vector is.
Students know how to write all vectors in 2D and 3D using unit vectors.

Success Criteria

I CAN….
Recall and explain a position vector.
Explain a unit vector.
Write the vector in component and basis form for R2 and R3 
Solve the magnitude of the vectors in R2 and R3 

Learning Goals

Students can define any vector 2D and 3D using a combination of unit vectors.

Success Criteria

I CAN….
Recall and explain collinear vectors.
Explain and perform linear combination of vectors for R2 and R3 
Explain spanning of the vectors in R2 and R3
