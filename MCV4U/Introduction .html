Introduction 

In this unit, we will delve into the fascinating world of vectors in two-space and three-space. You will develop a profound understanding of vectors both algebraically and geometrically, recognizing their practical applications in various contexts. We will equip you with the skills to perform operations on vectors in two-space and three-space, and you will leverage these operations to tackle real-world problems, sharpening your problem-solving capabilities.

Moreover, we will explore the intricate geometrical representations of single linear equations and systems of two linear equations in two-space and three-space. You will master the art of discerning diverse geometric configurations of lines and planes in three-space, providing you with a visual insight into the world of spatial relationships.

As a pivotal part of this unit, you will learn to represent lines and planes using scalar, vector, and parametric equations. These essential tools will empower you to solve problems related to distances and intersections, bridging the gap between abstract mathematical concepts and real-world scenarios.
