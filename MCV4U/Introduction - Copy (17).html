Optimization Problems Involving Exponential Functions

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will apply their understanding of derivative calculus to tackle optimization problems involving exponential functions. Building on their knowledge of derivatives, they will explore how these powerful mathematical tools can be used to optimize various real-world scenarios. By the end of this lesson, students will have the expertise to employ derivatives in solving optimization challenges related to exponential functions, enhancing their problem-solving skills and their ability to make informed decisions in practical situations.

Overall Expectations

B2. solve problems, including optimization problems, that require the use of the concepts and procedures associated with the derivative, including problems arising from real-world applications and involving the development of mathematical models.

 

Specific Expectations

B2.2 make connections between the graphical or algebraic representations of derivatives and real-world applications.
B2.4 solve optimization problems involving polynomial, simple rational, and exponential functions drawn from a variety of applications, including those arising from real-world situations.

Learning Goals

Students will know how to apply the derivatives of exponential functions to solve optimization problems.
