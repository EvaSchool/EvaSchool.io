Welcome to Calculus and Vectors – Grade 12 (MCV4U)

This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts of calculus and vector analysis. Students will learn to understand and apply derivatives, represent vectors in two and three dimensions, and solve real-world problems involving motion, optimization, and geometry.

MCV4U is designed for students planning to pursue university programs in mathematics, engineering, sciences, or economics. It builds essential skills for success in higher-level studies.

Unit Title Description
Unit One: Introduction to Derivatives Students will be introduced to the concept of a derivative through limits, average and instantaneous rates of change, and basic derivative rules for polynomial functions. Applications include curve sketching and basic motion problems.
Unit Two: Applications of Derivatives This unit extends students’ understanding of derivatives to real-world applications, including optimization, related rates, and more advanced motion analysis.
Unit Three: Introduction to Vectors Students will explore vector concepts in two and three dimensions, including magnitude, direction, operations with vectors, and applications to geometry and physics.
Unit Four: Lines and Planes In this unit, students will apply their knowledge of vectors to analyze lines and planes in 3D space. They will solve problems involving vector equations, intersections, and distances between geometric elements.
