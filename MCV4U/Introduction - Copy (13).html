Getting Started

Lesson Description

In this lesson, we're taking what you already know about exponential and trigonometric functions and digging deeper into the world of calculus. You'll explore how to find the derivatives of these functions, building on what you're already familiar with. This will help you become even better at solving problems, and you'll get a deeper understanding of these important math tools. By the end of the lesson, you'll be more confident in dealing with derivatives for exponential and trigonometric functions, which will come in handy for more advanced math challenges.

Learning Goals

  • Students will be familiar with exponential and trigonometric functions.
