The Derivative of Trigonometric Functions

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will acquire the skills to calculate derivatives for a variety of trigonometric functions, including sine, cosine, and tangent functions. They will explore the intricacies of these functions and master the techniques for finding their derivatives. By the end of this lesson, students will be well-versed in determining derivatives for trigonometric functions, enhancing their mathematical toolkit for solving a range of real-world problems and mathematical challenges.

Overall Expectations

A2. Graph the derivatives of polynomial, sinusoidal, and exponential functions, and make connections between the numerical, graphical, and algebraic representations of a function and its derivative.
