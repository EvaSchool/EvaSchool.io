Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will sharpen their skills in simplifying polynomial and rational expressions, equipping them with powerful tools for mathematical problem-solving. They'll explore techniques to streamline complex expressions and make them more manageable.

Additionally, students will delve into the world of lines and slopes. They'll learn how to find the slopes of parallel and perpendicular lines, an essential skill in understanding relationships between different lines in geometry and algebra. By the end of this lesson, students will have a clear understanding of these concepts, making their mathematical journey smoother and more insightful.

Learning Goals

  • Students will be able to simplify polynomial and rational expressions.
  • Students will be able to find the slopes of parallel and perpendicular lines
