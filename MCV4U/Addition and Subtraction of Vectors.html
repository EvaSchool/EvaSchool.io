What is a Vector?

A vector is a mathematical quantity having both magnitude and direction.

A vector can be represented by a directed line segment, a directed line segment has a length, called its magnitude, and a direction indicated by an arrowhead.

If vectors have direction, does that mean we may have opposite vectors?

Opposite Vectors: Two vectors that are opposites have the same magnitude but point in opposite directions.
