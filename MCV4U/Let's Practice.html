Answer:

The maximum height occurs when the velocity of the ball is zero—that is, when the slope of the tangent to the graph is zero. 

The velocity function is v(t) = s'(t) = -10t +30

Solving v(t) = 0, we obtain t = 3. This is the moment when the ball changes direction from up to down.
