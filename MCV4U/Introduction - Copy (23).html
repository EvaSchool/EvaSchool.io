Introduction 

In this unit, students will showcase their comprehension of vectors in both two-space and three-space through the representation of vectors algebraically and geometrically, with a keen awareness of their practical applications. They will adeptly perform operations on vectors in these spaces, utilizing the properties of these operations to solve problems, including those rooted in real-world scenarios.

Students will also distinguish between the geometric representations of a single linear equation or a system of two linear equations in two-space and three-space, identifying various geometric configurations of lines and planes in three-space. Furthermore, they will learn to represent lines and planes using scalar, vector, and parametric equations and apply this knowledge to problem-solving situations involving distances and intersections.
