Getting Started

Lesson Description

Students can sketch graphs of functions, solve equations, find the area of circles, find the volume and surface area of cubes, and identify maximum and minimum values using graphs of functions.

Overall Expectations

  • Make connections between concept of motion and the concept of derivatives
  • Solve problems involving rates of change
  • Determine  second derivatives and extreme values of a function
  • Solve problems by applying mathematical models and their derivatives to determine,
  • interpret  and communicate the mathematical results
  • Solve problems by determining the maximum and minimum values of a mathematical model.
