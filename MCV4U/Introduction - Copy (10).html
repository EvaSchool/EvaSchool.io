Introduction to The Derivative Function             

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will dive deep into the world of calculus. They'll begin by unraveling the definition of derivatives using the limit function, providing them with a foundational understanding of how functions change instantaneously. Building on this knowledge, students will master the art of using slopes of tangents to find derivatives, a practical skill that empowers them to explore and analyze the dynamic nature of functions. By the end of this lesson, students will be well-equipped to navigate the intricate concepts of calculus and apply them to real-world problem-solving.

Overall Outcomes

A2. Graph the derivatives of polynomial, sinusoidal, and exponential functions, and make connections between the numerical, graphical, and algebraic representations of a function and its derivative.

 

Specific Outcomes

A2.3 Determine the derivatives of polynomial functions by simplifying the algebraic expression and then taking the limit of the simplified expression as h approaches zero.

Learning Goals

  • Students will know the definition of a derivative using the limit function. 
  • Students will know how to use slopes of a tangent to find the derivative.
