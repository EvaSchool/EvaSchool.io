Unit 1: Matter, Chemical Trends and Chemical Bonding

Learning Goals

In this unit, the students will be able to:

Analyze the properties of commonly used chemical substances and their effects on human health and the environment and propose ways to lessen their impact.

Investigate the physical and chemical properties of elements and compounds and use various methods to visually represent them.

Demonstrate an understanding of periodic trends in the periodic table and how elements combine to form chemical bonds.
