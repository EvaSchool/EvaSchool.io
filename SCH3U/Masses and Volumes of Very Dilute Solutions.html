Masses and Volumes of Very Dilute Solutions

Callout

“Parts per” concentrations are a special case of % W/W concentration. Since a very dilute aqueous solution is very similar to pure water, its density is assumed to be 1 g/mL at 20 °C. 

Therefore, a solute concentration of 1 ppm means that there is 1 g of solute in every 1 000 000 g or 1 000 000 mL of solution.
