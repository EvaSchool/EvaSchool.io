Accordion

Lack of clarity

  • Ensure that the solution is clearly defined and easy to understand.

Inadequate research

  • Conduct comprehensive research to gather relevant data and insights before proposing a solution.

Limited feasibility

  • Consider the feasibility of implementing the solution within the given constraints, such as budget, time, and resources.

Unintended consequences

  • Evaluate potential unintended consequences of the solution and develop contingency plans to mitigate risks.

Resistance to Change

  • Anticipate resistance from stakeholders affected by the solution and involve them in the decision-making process to gain their support.
