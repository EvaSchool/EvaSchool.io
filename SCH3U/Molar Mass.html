Molar Mass

  • mass in grams of 1 mole or 6.02 X 1023 entities of that substance.
  • The units of molar mass are g/mol. 
  • Chemists often use the symbol M to represent molar mass, with the chemical symbol or formula as a subscript. The symbol for the molar mass of water is therefore

The molar mass of a compound is the sum of the molar mass of each entity in the compound.

Remember that you have to consider every element in the molecular formula, or formula unit, as you calculate the molar mass.
