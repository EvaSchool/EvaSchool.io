That's a Wrap

Learning Goals

In this lesson, students will be able to:

Learn about Combustion of Hydrocarbons

Identify the types of Hydrocarbons Combustion and concerns related to incomplete combustion

Review Acids and Bases

Identify the properties of Oxides

Define Green Chemistry

Understand the principles of Green Chemistry 

Identify applications of green chemistry in industry

Identify applications of the principles of green chemistry to pulp and paper

Success Criteria

Students will be successful if:

understand about Combustion of Hydrocarbons

identify the types of Hydrocarbons Combustion and concerns related to incomplete combustion

Identify the types of Neutralization Reactions and its applications to daily lives

Define Green Chemistry

Understand the principles of Green Chemistry

Identify applications of green chemistry in industry

Identify applications of the principles of green chemistry to pulp and paper
