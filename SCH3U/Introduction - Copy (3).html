Unit 3: Quantities in Chemical Reactions

Learning Goals

Welcome to Unit 3:

In this Unit you should achieve the following learning goals:

  • Differentiate Qualitative and Quantitative Analysis
  • Understand the importance of accuracy in industrial chemical processes and its potential impact in the environment if quantitative accuracy is not observed.
  • Learn the issues in chemical quantities
  • Learn about Mole
  • Solve Mole problems
  • Learn about Molar Mass
  • Determine the molar mass of an element and a compound.
  • Solve problems involving Molar masses
  • Learn about Mass and the number of Entities
  • Determine the number of entities
  • Understanding Combustion Analysis and
  • Percentage Composition
  • Solve Problems involving Percentage Composition
  • Understanding Drug-Contaminated Currency
  • Understanding Mole ratios
  • Determine ratios in chemical equations
  • Understand Masses and Chemical Equations
  • Understand Stoichiometric Amounts
  • Use stoichiometry in solving for mass
  • Learn about Limiting and Excess Reagents
  • Identify limiting reagents in chemical reactions
  • Identify applications of limiting reagents
  • Determine Percentage Yield
