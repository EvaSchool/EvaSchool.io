Molar Volume

What is Molar Volume?

  • The volume that one mole of gas occupies at a specified temperature and pressure
  •  One mole of any gas at standard temperature and pressure (STP, 0 °C and 101.325 kPa) occupies 22.4 L
  • One mole of any gas at standard ambient temperature and pressure (SATP, 25 °C and 100 kPa) occupies 24.8 L
