That's a Wrap

Learning Goals

In this lesson students will be able to:

Learn about Molecular Compounds

 

Identify the sources of Molecular Compounds

 

Understand about Upcycling

Understand about Bond and Molecular Polarity

Explain the behavior of Polar Liquids

Differentiate Ionic from Covalent Bond and Polar and Non-Polar

Compare Bonds and Intermolecular Forces

Identify the physical properties and types of Intermolecular Forces

Identify the special properties of water and surface tension

Learn about Green Chemistry

 

Understand the green alternatives and the aims of green chemistry
