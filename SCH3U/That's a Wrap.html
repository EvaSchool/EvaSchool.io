That's a Wrap

 

Learning Goals

In this lesson students should achieve the following learning goals:

  • Identify what chemistry is all about.
  • Identify international decision making bodies in chemistry.
  • Discuss how chemical knowledge of the atom has progressed.
  • Know how to draw Bohr-Rutherford diagrams.
  • Know how to determine atomic mass numbers, proton counts and electron counts of neutral atoms.
  • Understand the octet rule with ion formation.
  • Use Bohr diagrams to explain the formation of ions.
  • Name multivalent cations.
  • Name polyatomic anions.
  • Understand Isotopes and calculate atomic mass.
  • Understand radioisotopes and what decays they can undergo.
  • Know trends on the periodic table.
  • Use the periodic table to know if an element is a metal, non-metal or metalloid.
