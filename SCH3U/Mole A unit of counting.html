What is a Mole?

MOLE (unit symbol: mol)

  • the SI base unit for the amount of a substance.
  • the amount of substance containing 6.02 x 1023 entities;

AVOGADRO’S CONSTANT (NA)

  • the number of entities in 1 mol of a substance
