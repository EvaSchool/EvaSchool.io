Isotopes and trends in the periodic table

Isotopes, Radioisotopes, and Atomic Mass

Isotopes are elements (a fixed number of protons) with different numbers of neutrons. As neutrons have mass this will cause the nucleus to have a different mass. 

Radiation and Radioisotopes

Some atomic nuclei are unstable. The ratio of neutrons to protons can not stay together and the nucleus splits apart in a process known as nuclear decay.
