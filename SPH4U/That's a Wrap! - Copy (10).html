Learning Goals

  • Understand experimental evidence of the wave-particle duality.
  • Understand how particles of light act as classical particles in a way. 
  • Know about momentum transfer and collisions, how photons are produced, and how we use these effects in our daily lives.
  • Understand the uncertainty principle of quantum mechanics and the wave nature of electrons.
  • Understand the different models of the atom.
  • Understand how and be introduced to the standard model of particle physics.

  

Success Criteria

By the end of this lesson, I can…

  • Describe the experimental evidence of the wave-particle duality.
  • Explain how particles of light act as classical particles in a way.  
  • Describe momentum transfer and collisions, how photons are produced, and how we use these effects in our daily lives.
  • Explain the uncertainty principle of quantum mechanics and the wave nature of electrons.
  • Describe the different models of the atom.
  • Explain the standard model of particle physics.
