Learning Goals

  • To classify different types of forces in terms of contact and non-contact forces.
  • To represent forces within a coordinate system.
  • To find the net force acting on an object.
  • Understand what Newton’s 3 laws are and how we apply them to simple problems.
  • Be able to apply Newton’s Laws to equilibrium and dynamical problems in the real world.
  • Understand how friction interacts with dynamical bodies within Newton’s Force Laws.
  • Understand the forces acting on a skier.
  • Understand what the factors are affecting the motion of the skier.
