Learning Goals

Understand the underlying principles of special relativity and discover how frames of reference are used in a relativistic context.

Understand how time is observed differently in different reference frames.

Understand how length is perceived differently in different reference frames.

Understand that simultaneous events are not what you might think once we explore the twin paradox.

Relativistic momentum is also introduced and this brings into sharp focus the idea of a universal speed limit to travel.

Understand how mass and energy are related and the consequences of this fact.
