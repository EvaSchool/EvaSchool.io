Centripetal Force

Continue Learning

A net force that acts on an object to keep it moving in a circular path and it is directed towards the centre of the circular path.

Centripetal force causes objects to have a centripetal acceleration.
For a person swings a yo-yo with a string around his head, the force of tension across the string is the centripetal force. 
This tension keeps the yo-yo moving in a circular path.
If you were to let go of the yo-yo, the yo-yo will no longer move in a circular path.
