Learning Goals

  • Understand how forces can do work which is a form of energy transfer.
  • Be able to formalize the way work represents a change in the flow of energy.
  • Understand how gravity has the potential to do work on an object. 
  • Understand how energy is conserved in an isolated system.
  • Understand how we can use this in dynamical calculations.
  • Explore the motion of oscillations within a system.
  • Study it within the scope of simple harmonic motion.
  • Apply the ideas we found for simple harmonic motion to that of a spring in dynamic situations.
