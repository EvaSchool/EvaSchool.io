Unit 2 Summative

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will complete an assessment of your learning for this unit that is provided by the teacher.

Overall Expectations

  • C1 Demonstrate an understanding of the impact of norms, roles, and social institutions on individuals throughout the lifespan;

Specific Expectations

  • C1.1 Assess ways in which norms, values, and expectations (e.g., cultural or social norms and expectations, gender expectations, familial values and expectations) influence individual decisions throughout the lifespan (e.g., with regard to educational choices, career choices, choices about sexuality and relationships)
  • C1.2 Explain how multiple roles (e.g., student, volunteer, employee, friend, son/daughter, sibling, caregiver) can create personal conflict
  • C1.3 Analyse the impact of social institutions (e.g., the family; the media; educational, religious, economic, and political institutions) on the socialization of individuals throughout the lifespan

Learning Goals

  • Complete an overall assessment of your learning for this unit.
  • Communicate your learning in a variety of ways.
  • Demonstrate your understanding of theoretical perspectives and research on various aspects of individual development.
