That's a Wrap!

What have I learned?

In conclusion, the nature versus nurture debate, explored in this lesson, underscores the challenge of distinguishing between the effects of genetics and environment. Assessing research on sex and gender differences, considering the impact of cultural shifts on gender expectations, and analyzing the pervasive influence of norms and values on individual decisions throughout the lifespan are essential components of this nuanced understanding. Additionally, recent demographic trends, such as educational attainment and workforce participation, offer insights into the evolving landscape individuals navigate. Simultaneously, recognizing the impact of current social trends, from electronic media to climate change, contributes to a holistic comprehension of individual development, providing a robust foundation for addressing the complexities of human growth in the face of contemporary challenges.

Learning Goals

  • Assess research on sex-based similarities and differences as well as gender-based similarities and differences.
  • Assess ways in which norms, values, and expectations influence individual decisions throughout the lifespan.
  • Analyse the significance of recent demographic trends relating to the lives of individuals.
  • Assess the impact of current social trends, issues, and challenges on individual development.

Success Criteria

You are successful in this lesson if you...

  • Can assess research on sex-based similarities and differences as well as gender-based similarities and differences with reference to the com- plexity of distinguishing the effects of nature and nurture.
  • Can assess research on the impact of cultural, social, and historical change on expectations associated with gender.
  • Can assess ways in which gender expectations influence individual decisions throughout the lifespan.
  • Can analyse the significance of recent demographic trends relating to the lives of Canadian women in terms of income and workforce participation.
  • Can assess the impact of current social trends, issues, and challenges on Canadian women and education.
