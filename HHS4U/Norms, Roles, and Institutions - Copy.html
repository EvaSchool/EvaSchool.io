Individual Development and the Transition to Adulthood

An individual’s development is a gradual process that continues throughout one’s life. One of the most distinct and significant stages of an individual’s life or development is the transition to adulthood.

This stage is impacted by many life factors. The society that you are born into and the one in which you reside have an impact on your life in several ways.
