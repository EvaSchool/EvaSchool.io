Norms, Roles, and Institutions

Overall Expectations

C1 The Effects on Individuals: demonstrate an understanding of the impact of norms, roles, and social institutions on individuals throughout the lifespan.

Specific Expectations

  • C1.1 Assess ways in which norms, values, and expectations (e.g., cultural or social norms and expectations, gender expectations, familial values and expectations) influence individual decisions throughout the lifespan (e.g., with regard to educational choices, career choices, choices about sexuality and relationships).
  • C1.3 Analyse the impact of social institutions (e.g., the family; the media; educational, religious, economic, and political institutions) on the socialization of individuals throughout the lifespan.

Learning Goals

  • Students will assess ways in which norms, values, and expectations influence individual decisions throughout the lifespan.
  • Students will analyse the impact of social institutions on the socialization of individuals throughout the lifespan.
  • Students will reflect on the impact of social norms, roles, and institutions on the personal level.
