Trends, Issues, And Challenges

Lesson Description

In this lesson on Trends, Issues, and Challenges, students will delve into the art of analyzing and interpreting research information. They will learn how to compare results from diverse sources, extract common themes, and differentiate primary data findings from secondary source reports. Moreover, students will gain the skills to effectively communicate their research results in various formats tailored to specific purposes and audiences. This lesson equips students with vital research and communication proficiencies to understand, discuss, and address contemporary trends, issues, and challenges.

Overall Expectations

A3 Processing Information: assess, record, analyze, and synthesize information gathered through research and inquiry.

A4 Communicating and Reflecting: communicate the results of their research and inquiry clearly and effectively, and reflect on and evaluate their research, inquiry, and communication skills.

Specific Expectations

A3.3 Analyse and interpret research information (e.g., compare results of interviews with different groups; determine whether common themes arise in different sources; compare results from primary data sources with results reported in secondary sources).

A4.1 Use an appropriate format (e.g., oral presentation, poster, multimedia presentation, research report, web page) to communicate the results of their research and inquiry effectively for a specific purpose and audience.

Learning Goals

  • Students will analyze and interpret research information.
  • Students will use an appropriate format to communicate the results of their research and inquiry effectively for a specific purpose and audience.
