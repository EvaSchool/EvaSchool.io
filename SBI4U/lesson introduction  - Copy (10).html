Introduction to Anaerobic Pathways

Overall Expectations

C2.  investigate the products of metabolic processes such as cellular respiration; 

C3. Demonstrate an understanding of the chemical changes and energy conversions that occur in metabolic processes.  

Specific Expectations

C3.1 explains the chemical changes and energy conversions associated with the processes of aerobic and anaerobic cellular respiration 

C3.3 uses the laws of thermodynamics to explain energy transfer in the cell during the processes of anaerobic cellular respiration

C3.4 describe, compare, and illustrate (e.g., using flow charts) the matter and energy transformations that occur during the processes of cellular respiration (anaerobic), including the roles of oxygen and organelles such as mitochondria and chloroplasts 

Learning Goals

  1. explain the chemical changes and energy conversions associated with the processes of aerobic and anaerobic cellular respiration 
  2. use the laws of thermodynamics to explain energy transfer in the cell during the processes of anaerobic cellular respiration
  3. describe, compare, and illustrate (e.g., using flow charts) the matter and energy transformations that occur during the processes of cellular respiration (anaerobic), including the roles of oxygen and organelles such as mitochondria and chloroplasts 

Success Criteria

  1. Identify the different types of carbohydrates and lipids.

  2. Draw the structures of carbohydrates and lipids discussed here.
