Photosynthesis and Cellular Respiration: A Comparison

Overall Expectations

C.3 demonstrates an understanding of the chemical changes and energy conversions that occur in metabolic processes.

Specific Expectations

C.3.1 Explains the chemical changes and energy conversions associated with the processes of aerobic and anaerobic cellular respiration.

C.3.2 Explains the chemical changes and energy conversions associated with the process of photosynthesis.

C.3.3 Describe, compare, and illustrate the matter and energy transformations that occur during the processes of cellular respiration and photosynthesis, including the roles of oxygen and organelles such as mitochondria and chloroplasts.

Learning Goals

Understand the chemical changes and energy conversions in photosynthesis and cellular respiration.

Differentiate aerobic respiration from anaerobic respiration.

Explain the roles of oxygen and organelles such as mitochondria and chloroplast in the said metabolic processes.

Success Criteria

By the end of this lesson, I can…

  1. Understand the chemical changes and energy conversions in photosynthesis and cellular respiration.

  2. Differentiate aerobic respiration from anaerobic respiration.

  3. Explain the roles of oxygen and organelles such as mitochondria and chloroplast in the said metabolic processes.
