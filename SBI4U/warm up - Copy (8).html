In what way does the presence of water affect photosynthesis?

Flip

The presence of water positively influences photosynthesis by serving as a crucial reactant in the process. Water is a raw material in the light-dependent reactions of photosynthesis, where it is split during photolysis to release oxygen and provide electrons for the formation of energy-rich molecules. Additionally, water helps maintain turgor pressure in plant cells, supporting overall cellular structure and function essential for efficient photosynthesis.
