"Did you know that the remarkable distinction between living organisms and inert matter lies in the existence of cells?

Cells are the fundamental building blocks of life, and every living entity, from petunias and people to the tiniest viruses, is composed of these intricate cellular structures. In contrast, stones, rocks, and sand lack this vital cellular foundation. It's the presence of cells that brings life to the world around us."
