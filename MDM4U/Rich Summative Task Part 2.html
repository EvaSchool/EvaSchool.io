Rich Summative Task Part 2 

 

Learning Goals

  • Critique a game of chance and the related analysis.
  • Complete the second part of your RST.

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will continue the culminating investigations for this course by critiquing another game of chance along with your own and giving some analysis. To complete this part of your RST, you should:

  1. Ask your teacher to give you the RST Part 2 Instructions PDF.
  2. Follow the “Investigate Analyzing a Culminating Probability Project” instructions. Discuss the 9 Conclusion questions from this section with your teacher.
  3. Follow the “Step 4: Planning Your Report” instructions. You need to record your answers for this.
  4. Follow the “Step 5: Writing Your Report” instructions by compiling your answers to Step 1, Step 2, Step 3, and Step 4 from Part 1 and Part 2 of your RST to create a formal report.
  5. Follow the “Step 6: Finalizing Your Project” instructions to complete your report.
  6. Follow the “Step 7: Assessing Your Probability Project” instructions by filling in the self-assessment checklist for your work.
  7. Notify your teacher once you have completed these steps.

 

Homework 

Complete this part of your RST and notify your teacher once you are ready for Part 3.
