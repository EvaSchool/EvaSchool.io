Rich Summative Task Part 5 

 

Learning Goals

  • Critique your research report or presentation constructively.
  • Critique the research reports or presentations of my peers based on predetermined criteria.

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will continue the culminating investigations for this course by critiquing your research report as well as the reports of peers or past reports. 

provided by your teacher. Then you will plan your own game of chance. To complete this part of your RST, you should:

  1. Ask your teacher to give you the RST Part 5 Instructions PDF.
  2. Follow the “Assessing Your Culminating Probability Project” instructions. Record yourself presenting your project and report from RST Part 1 and 2 to your teacher and other students. Ask your teacher for recordings from other students to critique.
  3. Discuss the three reflection questions with your teacher.
  4. Follow the “Assessing Your Culminating Statistics Project” instructions. Record your project and report from RST Part 3 and 4 to your teacher and other students. Ask your teacher for recordings from other students to critique.
  5. Discuss the three reflection questions with your teacher.
  6. Notify your teacher once you have completed these steps.
