Pascal’s Triangle:

A triangular array of numbers in which each term is the sum of two terms above it.

Pascal’s Method: 

The terms of Pascal’s triangle are generated by adding two adjacent terms and placing the result immediately below them in the next row.

Pascal’s Triangle

Pascal’s Triangle is shown, along with the term references. The terms are designated by tn,r, where n is the row number, starting at zero, and r is the diagonal number, also starting at zero.
