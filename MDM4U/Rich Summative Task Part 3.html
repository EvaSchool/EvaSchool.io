Rich Summative Task Part 3

 

Learning Goals

  • Pose research questions and state hypotheses.
  • Design a plan to investigate a research question.
  • Gather, organize, and represent data.

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will continue the culminating investigations for this course by posing research questions in order to form hypotheses. You will then design a plan to investigate these hypotheses and finally gather, organize and represent data of your choice. To complete this part of your RST, you should:

  1. Ask your teacher to give you the RST Part 3 Instructions PDF.
  2. Follow the “Step 1: Defining and Posing the Problem” instructions. You need to record your answers for this. Use the “Example 1” to help you form your questions.
  3. Follow the “Step 2: Finding and Collecting Secondary Data” instructions by researching the data needed for you to answer your questions from Step 1. Use “Example 2” to help you with this section.
  4. Follow the “Step 3: Gathering Primary Data” instructions and record your answers.
  5. Follow the “Step 4: Graphing Your Data” instructions and record your answers including your graph(s).
  6. Follow the “Step 5: Assessing Your Project So Far” to fill in the self-assessment checklist. This is meant to keep you on track to complete all the aspects of this part of your RST.
  7. Notify your teacher once you have completed these steps.

 

Homework 

Complete this part of your RST and notify your teacher once you are ready for Part 4.
