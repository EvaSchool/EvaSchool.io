Hypergeometric Distributions

Minds On

The binomial distribution involves independent trials. This section develops a distribution involving dependent trials. Cutting cards with a standard deck provides independent trials, whereas dealing the cards involves dependent trials. Similarly, selecting a jury pool and catching and tagging animals for scientific research involve dependent trials. Brainstorm other examples of dependent trials.

Concepts

Hypergeometric Probability Distribution

  • a distribution with dependent trials whose outcomes are either success or failure
  • the random variable is the number of successes in a given number of trials

Example

A committee of six people is to be formed from a pool of six grade 11 students and seven grade 12 students. Determine the probability that the committee will have two grade 11 students.
