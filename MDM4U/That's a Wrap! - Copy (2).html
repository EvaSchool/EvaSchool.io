Learning Goals

  • Recognize and identify a discrete random variable.
  • Generate a probability distribution for all the values of a random variable.
  • Solve problems involving uniform probability distributions.
  • Recognize situations that give rise to a binomial distribution.
  • Recognize conditions of, calculate the probability of, and use charts and tables to represent.
  • Solve real-world problems involving, hypergeometric distributions.
  • Compare the probability distribution of different types of data.
  • Solve problems involving uniform, binomial, and hypergeometric probability distributions.
