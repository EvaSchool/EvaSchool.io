Combinations

Definition of Terms

Combinations

 A selection from a group of objects without regard to order.

For example, consider a set with the three elements, A, B, and C. The combinations of these three elements taken two at a time are:
AB       AC     BA       BC      CA       CB 

We notice that the number of combinations is a lot fewer than the number of permutations.
