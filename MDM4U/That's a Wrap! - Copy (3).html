That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

  • Identify different types of data.

  • Analyze how data can be misused, or misrepresented, by the media.

  • Determine the difference between a population and a sample.

  • Analyze how sample data can be used to determine things about a population.

  • Design effective surveys to collect data.

  • Analyze primary and secondary sources of data, as well as microdata vs aggregate data.

  • Collect and analyze primary and secondary data.

  • Identify the different types of bias that exist in surveys. 

  • Analyze ways that the media and other sources represent data in misleading ways to fit their agenda.

Success Criteria

I can ...

  • Identify the different types of data
  • Examine how data can be misused in statistical studies
  • Differentiate population and sample
  • Analyze how sample data can be used to determine things about the population
  • Make effective surveys to gather data
  • Analyze primary and secondary sources of data,  microdata, and aggregate data
  • Gather and analyze primary and secondary data
  • Identify the various types of bias in surveys
  • Examine how the media and other sources deliver data in misleading ways according to their agenda
