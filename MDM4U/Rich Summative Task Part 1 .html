Rich Summative Task Part 1 

 

Learning Goals

  • Assess games for elements of chance or randomness and critique them for fairness.
  • Plan a game of chance for a culminating probability project.

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will begin the culminating investigations for this course by assessing games for elements of chance and critique them for fairness. Then you will plan your own game of chance. To complete this part of your RST, you should:

  1. Ask your teacher to give you the RST Part 1 Instructions PDF.
  2. Follow the “Investigate a Game of Chance” instructions. If you do not have access to a group of people to help you, play by yourself taking multiple turns for the other imaginary people. Discuss these steps with your teacher.
  3. Follow the “Investigate Creating a Game of Chance” instructions. Work alone if you do not have access to a group of people. Discuss these steps with your teacher.
  4. Follow the “Step 1: Prepping for Game Construction” instructions. You need to record your answers for this.
  5. Follow the “Step 2: Assessing Your Plan” instructions by filling in the checklist (take a screenshot of the PDF and use a markup tool if necessary).
  6. Follow the “Step 3: Collecting Your Data” instructions and record your findings.
  7. Notify your teacher once you have completed these steps.

 

Homework 

Complete this part of your RST and notify your teacher once you are ready for Part 2.
