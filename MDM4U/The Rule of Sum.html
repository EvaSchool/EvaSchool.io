The Rule of Sum

Many grade 12 students consider attending university. Sidney is applying to Waterloo for math or engineering, Queen’s for physics, chemistry, or engineering, and Laurentian for science or engineering. How many program choices does Sidney have in total?

Jenna is applying to Windsor for business or economics, York for commerce or business, and Ottawa for economics, political, science, or business. How many program choices does Jenna have in total?

Describe how you calculated the number of choices Sidney and Jenna each have.

The rule of sum states that if one mutually exclusive event can occur in m ways, and a second can occur in n ways, then one or the other can occur in m + n ways.

Indirect Method

The indirect method: subtract the number of unwanted outcomes from the total number of outcomes without restrictions

Example

A security code consists of either five or six different letters. How many distinct security codes are possible?

There are 173 659 200 possible distinct security codes.
