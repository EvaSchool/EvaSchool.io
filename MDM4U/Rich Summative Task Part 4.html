Rich Summative Task Part 4 

 

Learning Goals

  • Represent and interpret one- and two-variable statistics from a data set.
  • Evaluate the impact of outliers on my calculations and justify my results.
  • Organize, summarize, and interpret the key results of your data analysis.

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will continue the culminating investigations for this course by interpreting the data you collected and summarized in Part 3. You will also analyze the impact of any outliers in your data and finally will summarize and interpret the key findings of your data analysis. To complete this part of your RST, you should:

  1. Ask your teacher to give you the RST Part 4 Instructions PDF.
  2. Follow the “Step 6: Digging Into the Centre of Your Data” instructions. You need to record your answers for this.
  3. Follow the “Step 7: Searching for Patterns” instructions. You need to record your answers for this.
  4. Follow the “Step 8: Assessing Your Data Analysis” instructions by filling in the checklist provided.
  5. Follow the “Step 9: Assessing the Validity of Your Process and Results” instructions. You need to record your answers for this.
  6. Follow the “Step 10: Assessing Your Results” instructions by filling in the checklist provided.
  7. Notify your teacher once you have completed these steps.

 

Homework
