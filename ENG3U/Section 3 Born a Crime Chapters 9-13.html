Section 3: Born a Crime Chapters 9-13

  

Born a Crime is not the first book to speak about the systematic issue of racial discrimination. 

Many talented authors have tackled this problem in their literary pieces with such unflinching honesty. 

  

Let’s continue reading Born a Crime, Chapters IX-XIII.
