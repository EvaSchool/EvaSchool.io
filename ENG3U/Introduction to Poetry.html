Introduction to Poetry

What you Need to Know About Poetry

  • Poetry is from the ancient Greek work poieo which means “I create.”
  • It is an art form in which human language is used for its aesthetic qualities in addition to, or instead of, its notional and semantic content.
  • Poetry consists largely of oral or literary works in which language is used in a manner that is felt by its user and audience to differ from ordinary prose.
