Simple and Continuous Verb Tenses  

Lesson Description

You will learn about simple and continuous verb tenses in this lesson.

Overall Expectations

C2 Using Knowledge of Form and Style: Draft and revise their writing, using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms and stylistic elements appropriate for the purpose and audience.

C3 Applying Knowledge of Conventions: Use editing, proofreading, and publishing skills and strategies, and knowledge of language conventions, to correct errors, refine expression, and present their work effectively.

Specific Expectations

C2.4 Write complete sentences that communicate their meaning clearly and accurately, varying sentence type, structure, and length for different purposes and making logical transitions between ideas.

C3.4 Use grammar conventions correctly to communicate their intended meaning clearly.

Learning Goals

  • Students will learn about the simple and continuous verb tenses.
  • Students will identify when to use the simple and continuous verb tenses.
  • Students will apply the simple and continuous verb tenses in their writing and the assigned tasks.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

 

  • I can explain the differences between simple and continuous verb tenses.
  • I can identify when to use simple and continuous verb tenses.
  • I can apply the simple and continuous verb tenses in their writing and the assigned tasks.
