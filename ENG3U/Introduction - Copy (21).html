Sonnets                      

Lesson Description

In the lesson, you will learn about the PACTS Short Story Analysis. You will also learn about the RACES strategy in answering comprehension questions. Then, you will apply these concepts independently.

Overall Expectations

D2 Understanding Media Forms, Conventions, and Techniques: Identify some media forms and explain how the conventions and techniques associated with them are used to create meaning.

Specific Expectations

D2.1 Identify general and specific characteristics of a variety of media forms and explain how they shape content and create meaning.

D2.2 Identify conventions and/or techniques used in a variety of media forms and explain how they convey meaning and influence their audience.

Learning Goals

  • Students will learn about the elements of a sonnet.
  • Students will understand the format of a sonnet.
  • Students will write a sonnet using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

 

  • I can identify the elements of a sonnet.
  • I can understand and explain the format of a sonnet.
  • I can write a sonnet using a variety of literary, informational, and graphic forms.
