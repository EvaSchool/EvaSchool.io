Analyzing Non-Fiction Essay

What elements do we need, in order to analyze a piece of nonfiction writing?

We need to know:

  1. Genre
  2. Audience
  3. Purpose

Genre

Genre is defined as: a distinctive type or category of literary composition.

Questions to Ask About the Text

  • What is the genre of this text? How do you know?
  • Where did the text come from?

Audience

Audience is defined as: the group of people who will be attracted to your writing. They may share certain subject interests, social or political beliefs, or certain demographic features

Questions to Ask About the Text:

  • Who is the text aimed for? How do you know?
  • A writer always has an audience in mind. Different styles of writing will be used for different types of audience

Purpose

  • Purpose is defined as: the reason for or intent in writing
  • What does the text want you to do?
  • How does the text make you do it?

    All documents have a purpose: some entertain, some inform, some persuade Non-fiction texts can be written for a variety of purposes.

For example a non-fiction text might be written to:

  • Provide instruction
  • Promote awareness
  • Inform about the facts
  • Report news
  • Persuade the reader
