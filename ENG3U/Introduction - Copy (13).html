The Communication Process in Advertisements

Lesson Description

You will learn about the communication process and identify its presence in advertisements through this lesson. You will examine the process in two advertisements, and then analyze one in the discussion forum.

Overall Expectations

D1 Understanding Media Texts: Demonstrate an understanding of a variety of media texts.

D2 Understanding Media Forms, Conventions, and Techniques: Identify some media forms and explain how the conventions and techniques associated with them are used to create meaning.

Specific Expectations

D1.1 Explain how media texts, including increasingly complex or difficult texts, are created to suit particular purposes and audiences.

D1.4 Explain why the same media text might prompt different responses from different audiences.

D2.1 Identify general and specific characteristics of a variety of media forms and explain how they shape content and create meaning.

Learning Goals

  • Students will identify the components of the communication process.
  • Students will explain the communication process used in advertisements.
  • Students identify general and specific characteristics of the communication process in advertisements and explain how they shape content and create meaning.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

 

  • I can identify the components of the communication process.
  • I can explain the communication process used with advertisements.
  • I can identify general and specific characteristics of the communication process in advertisements.
  • I can explain how the characteristics shape the content and create meaning.
