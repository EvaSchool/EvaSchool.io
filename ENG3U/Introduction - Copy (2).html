Literacy Devices

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will learn about literary devices through pop culture.

Overall Expectations

B1 Reading for Meaning: Read and demonstrate an understanding of literary devices using a range of strategies to construct meaning.

B2 Understanding Form and Style: Recognize literary devices and demonstrate how they help communicate meaning.

C2 Using Knowledge of Form and Style: Draft and revise your writing to explain how the literary device is present in the examples you have chosen.

C3 Applying Knowledge of Conventions: Use editing, proofreading, publishing skills and strategies, and knowledge of language conventions to correct errors, refine expression, and present your work effectively.

D1 Understanding Media Texts: demonstrate an understanding of a variety of media texts;

D3 Creating Media Texts: Create a Google Slides presentation using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.

Specific Expectations

B1.4 Identify the most important ideas and supporting details in texts, including increasingly complex or difficult texts.

B2.2 Identify a variety of elements of style in texts and explain how they help communicate meaning and enhance the effectiveness of the texts.

C2.4 Write complete sentences that communicate their meaning clearly and effectively, skillfully varying sentence types.

C3.4 Use grammar conventions correctly and appropriately to communicate their intended meaning clearly and effectively.

D1.2 interprets media texts, including increasingly complex or difficult texts, identifying and explaining the overt and implied messages they convey.

D3.4 Produce media texts, including increasingly complex texts, for a variety of purposes and audiences, using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques

Learning Goals

  • Apply understanding of literary devices to other forms of media
  • Read and demonstrate an understanding of literary devices using a range of strategies to construct meaning.
  • Recognize literary devices and demonstrate how they help communicate meaning.
  • Draft and revise your writing to explain how the literary device is present in the examples you have chosen.
  • Use editing, proofreading, publishing skills and strategies, and knowledge of language conventions to correct errors, refine expression, and present your work effectively.
  • Create a Google Slides presentation using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

  • I can apply my understanding of literary devices to other forms of media.
  • I can demonstrate an understanding of literary devices using a range of strategies to construct meaning.
  • I can recognize literary devices and demonstrate how they help communicate meaning.
  • I can draft and revise my writing to explain my ideas.
  • I can use editing, proofreading, publishing skills and strategies, and knowledge of language conventions to correct errors, refine expression, and present my work effectively.
  • I can create a Google Slides presentation using appropriate forms, conventions, and techniques.
