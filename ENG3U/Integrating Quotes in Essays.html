Quotations

Techniques: Direct Quotation

  • You do this primarily by directly quoting the author.
  • The lifted text is then placed in between quotation marks (“...”).

Techniques: Paraphrasing

  • This is your OWN version of the main idea and essential information.
  • It is more detailed than a summary, which focuses only on the main idea.
  • Paraphrasing also helps you understand the meaning of the original text.

Techniques: Summarization

  • This involves rephrasing the main idea and the main points using your own words.
  • In summarizations, page numbers are not usually needed since the work is being discussed as a whole rather than selecting a passage from it.
