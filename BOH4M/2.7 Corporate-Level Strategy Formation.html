Corporate-Level Strategy Formation

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will learn and understand corporate-level strategy.

Overall Expectations

D3. Analyse the relationship between strategic planning and the success of an organization.

Specific Expectations

D3.1 Explain the importance of the strategic planning process and describe the levels of strategic planning (corporate, business, functional) in an organization.

D3.2 Describe the types of strategic plans (e.g., growth, retrenchment, e-business) used by specific organizations.

D3.3 Analyse organizational strategic plans, using a variety of management tools.

D3.4 Analyse the two levels of corporate culture (core and observable) and describe the relationship between corporate culture and strategic planning.

Learning Goals

  • Students will learn corporate-level strategy.
  • Students will know the importance of corporate-level strategy formation.
  • Students will know what global strategies are.
