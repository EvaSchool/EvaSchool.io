Foundations of Management


Welcome to the world of management! In this Unit, we'll explore the fundamental principles that govern managing organizations. We'll delve into the evolution of management practices, examine core functions, and illuminate the hierarchical structure of management. We'll pay homage to visionaries who shaped modern management theory and analyze the latest workplace trends. Effective communication is crucial in successful management, and we'll emphasize its pivotal role. Join us on an enlightening journey through management, empowering you with knowledge and skills to navigate its ever-evolving landscape!
