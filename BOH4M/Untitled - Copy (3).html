Communication

This competency involves the ability to share ideas and findings clearly through written and oral expression. It includes skills such as writing, oral presentation, giving and receiving feedback, and effectively utilizing technology for communication.

Teamwork

Teamwork competency is about working effectively as both a team member and a team leader. It encompasses skills related to contributing to a team, leading teams, managing conflicts, negotiating, and building consensus.

Self-Management

Self-management competency involves the ability to evaluate oneself, modify behavior, and meet performance obligations. It includes areas like ethical reasoning and behavior, personal flexibility, tolerance for ambiguity, and taking responsibility for one's performance.

Leadership

Leadership competency is about influencing and supporting others to perform complex and ambiguous tasks. It includes skills related to diversity awareness, understanding global contexts, project management, and taking strategic actions.

Critical Thinking

Critical thinking competency focuses on the ability to gather and analyze information for creative problem-solving. It includes skills such as problem-solving, judgment and decision-making, information gathering and interpretation, as well as fostering creativity and innovation.

Professionalism

Professionalism competency is essential for sustaining a positive impression, instilling confidence, and managing one's career advancement. It involves personal presence, taking initiative, and actively managing one's career path.
