Managing Planned Change

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will evaluate different strategies used by manager to bring about acceptance of planned change.

Overall Expectations

D4. Analyse how companies respond to internal and external pressures for change.

Specific Expectations

D4.1 Analyse the elements that bring about change in an organization.

D4.2 Analyse the reasons for various attitudes towards change (e.g., why individuals fear change, why individuals embrace change).

D4.3 Evaluate different strategies used by man­ agers to bring about acceptance of planned change (e.g., education, shared power, negotiation).

D4.4 Analyse major challenges (e.g., new tech­nologies, the influence of the Internet, globalization) and legal considerations (e.g., workplace safety standards, access for people with disabilities, pay equity) facing today's organizations.

Learning Goals

  • Students will evaluate different strategies used by managers to bring about acceptance of planned change.
  • Students will analyze major challenges and legal considerations facing today's organization.
