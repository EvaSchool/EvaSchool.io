Implementing Plans to Achieve Results

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will learn the characteristics of a great goal.

Overall Expectations

D1. Analyze the importance of planning to the success of an organization.

D2. Demonstrate an understanding of appropriate planning tools and techniques in a variety of situations.

Specific Expectations

D1.1 Describe the planning process and its bene­ fits to the organization (e.g., flexibility, coordination, time management).

D1.2 Apply different problem-solving strategies to a variety of management planning challenges.

D1.3 Analyse the importance of individual and group creativity in planning.

D2.1 Describe tools and strategies used in the planning process. 

D2.2 Explain the use of effective short-term planning tools and strategies (e.g., policies, operational plans, planning approaches) and long-term planning tools (e.g., bud­ gets, strategic plans, simulations, forecasts).

D2.3 Demonstrate the ability to use time-management techniques.

Learning Goals

  • Understand how to implement a plan and achieve results. 
  • Learn the five characteristics of a great goal.
  • Know the importance of goal setting and goal alignment.
