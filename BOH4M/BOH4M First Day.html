BOH4M – First Day Welcome

BOH4M, Business Leadership: Management Fundamentals, Grade 12 University/College Preparation

Welcome to BOH4M!

Welcome to BOH4M! This course will help you develop the essential skills to become an effective leader and manager. Treat this course as you would a university class—read instructions carefully, reflect regularly on your learning, and aim to improve with every assignment. Stay curious, communicate clearly, and challenge yourself—your efforts will be recognized.

The course emphasizes the fundamental functions of management (planning, organizing, leading, controlling), effective communication, team dynamics, ethical decision-making, and problem-solving in real organizational contexts. You will apply leadership theories to case studies, projects, and simulations to develop practical skills for postsecondary studies and the workplace.

Prerequisite: None.

BOH4M will support your growth in leadership, communication, and project management. Through readings, discussions, role-play, and team projects, you will strengthen your ability to make decisions and take action in dynamic environments.

Success in BOH4M depends on your active participation and willingness to collaborate. Take time to reflect on feedback, set goals for improvement, and manage your time proactively. The skills you build here—strategy, teamwork, accountability—will serve you well in university and in your future career.
