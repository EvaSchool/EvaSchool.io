Control Tools and Techniques

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will learn what are the control tools and techniques. 

Overall Expectations

D5. Assess the importance of control in management.

Specific Expectations

D5.1 describe the steps in the control process and how they relate to the managerial process of leading, planning, and organizing;
D5.2 describe progressive discipline and its impact on the individual within an organization;
D5.3 describe the impact of internal control measures (e.g., self-discipline, goal setting) and external control measures (e.g., compensation systems, progressive discipline) on an organization;
D5.4 explain the three types of control systems used by organizations (feedforward, concurrent, feedback)

Learning Goals

  • Students will describe progressive discipline and its impact on the individual within an organization.
  • Students will describe the impact of internal control measures and external control measures on an organization;
  • Students will explain the three types of control systems used by organizations.
