Mission and Stakeholders

A clear mission is essential for inspiring stakeholders, including customers, shareholders, suppliers, creditors, and community groups. A strategic constituency analysis helps assess stakeholder interests and the organization's response.

Strategic Core Values

Core values and organizational culture play a crucial role in supporting the mission. They guide behavior and build organizational identity.

Organizational Culture

Organizational culture defines the atmosphere and values within an organization. It consists of observable culture and core culture, with both contributing to the organization's identity.

SWOT Analysis

SWOT analysis involves assessing organizational strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. Strengths should lead to sustainable competitive advantages, while weaknesses need to be addressed. Opportunities and threats are external factors that impact the organization.

PEST Analysis

PEST analysis considers the political, economic, socio-cultural, technological, and natural environmental conditions affecting the organization's decision-making.

Porter's Five Forces Model

Porter's Five Forces Model analyzes industry competition, new entrants, substitute products or services, bargaining power of suppliers, and bargaining power of customers to determine industry attractiveness.

 Industry Attractiveness

An attractive industry has less rivalry, fewer threats from new entrants or substitutes, and low bargaining power among suppliers and buyers. Industry structure influences the potential for sustained competitive advantage.

Strategic Analysis

Strategic analysis assesses the organization's resources, capabilities, competitive environment, and current strategies.

Strategy Formulation

Strategy formulation involves developing new or revised strategies to achieve sustainable competitive advantage.

Strategy Implementation

Strategy implementation uses resources to put strategies into action.

Evaluation

Evaluation involves assessing results and making adjustments as needed.
