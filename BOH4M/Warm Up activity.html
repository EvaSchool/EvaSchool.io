"Organization Olympics"

Objective: To introduce the concept of organizations in a fun and engaging way through a multiple-choice game.

Instructions:

  1. Imagine you are about to participate in the "Organization Olympics." In this game, you'll learn about organizations while having some fun!

  2. Read the following passage about organizations:

    "An organization is like a team of superheroes working together to save the world. It's a unique social phenomenon that allows its members to accomplish incredible feats that no individual could achieve alone. These organizations come in all shapes and sizes, from the mighty corporations that rule the business world to the local small businesses that keep your neighborhood vibrant. They even include non-profit organizations like schools, government agencies, and hospitals."
