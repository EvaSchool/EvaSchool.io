Why and How Managers Plan

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will understand why and how managers plan and learn the five steps of planning.

Overall Expectations

D1. Analyze the importance of planning to the success of an organization.

Specific Expectations

D1.1 Describe the planning process and its benefits to the organization (e.g., flexibility, coordination, time management).

D1.2 Apply different problem-solving strategies to a variety of management planning challenges.

D1.3 Analyse the importance of individual and group creativity in planning.

Learning Goals

  • Students will understand why and how managers plan.
  • Students will learn the five steps of planning.
  • Students will know the importance and benefits of planning.
