Long-Range and Short-Range Plans

Long-term plans used to look several years into the future, but the modern environment has made long-term planning less permanent and subject to frequent revisions. Would you agree that complexities and uncertainties challenge how far ahead we can plan?

Strategic and Tactical Plans

Strategic plans focus on the organization as a whole, setting broad action directions and goals. Tactical plans are developed to implement strategic plans and deal with current situations in ways that align with the overall strategy. Can you think of examples of strategic and tactical plans in the business world?

Functional Plans

Functional plans, such as production, financial, facilities, logistics, marketing, and human resource plans, specify how different components of an organization contribute to the overall strategy. These plans help ensure alignment across various functions. How important is cross-functional coordination in achieving organizational goals?

Operational Plans

Operational plans guide short-term behavior and support strategic and tactical plans. They include standing plans like policies and procedures, which provide guidelines and specific rules for decision-making and actions. How do policies and procedures contribute to the smooth functioning of an organization?

Budgets

Budgets are single-use plans that allocate resources for specific activities, projects, or programs within set time periods. Managers often negotiate for adequate budgets to support their work units or teams. How can effective budgeting impact an organization's performance?
