Team Structures

 

Team structures are a part of the broader move towards more horizontal organizational structures. In these structures, teams serve as the fundamental building blocks, both permanent and temporary.

Cross-functional teams are commonly used to break down functional barriers and improve lateral relations.

Team structures can boost communication, morale, and enthusiasm among employees.

However, they can also introduce complexities related to conflicting loyalties, time management, and group dynamics.

Boundaryless Structures

Boundaryless structures aim to eliminate internal boundaries among subsystems and external boundaries with the environment. They combine elements of team and network structures, emphasizing teamwork and communication while also incorporating "temporariness."

Boundaryless structures promote spontaneous communication and teamwork, replacing formal lines of authority.

However, they may face challenges related to complex team dynamics and time management.

Network Structures

 

Network structures involve a central core connected through networks of relationships with external contractors and service providers. Organizations focus on owning only core components and use strategic alliances and outsourcing for the rest.

Network structures are lean and streamlined, reducing overhead and increasing efficiency.

However, they require effective management of a complex network of contracts and alliances, and there can be challenges in maintaining control and loyalty among contractors.

Virtual Organizations

The virtual organization takes the boundaryless concept to the extreme. It operates as a shifting network of alliances engaged as needed, using information technology and the internet to eliminate traditional boundaries between the firm, suppliers, customers, and even competitors.

Virtual organizations are highly flexible and responsive, with alliances formed and disbanded as circumstances change.

However, they require effective management of dynamic alliances and may face issues related to control and loyalty.
