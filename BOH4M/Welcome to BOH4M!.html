Unit Topic Description
Unit 1

Foundations of Management

Continue Learning

Foundations of Management introduces you to the essential concepts of leadership and management. Throughout this unit, you will explore the challenges faced by contemporary workplace leaders, examine historical management theories that continue to shape our understanding today, acquire business and management terminology, analyze various organizational management structures, and dissect the elements of the management process. This foundational knowledge will lay the groundwork for your journey toward becoming a proficient and effective manager.
Unit 2

Planning

Continue Learning

In this Unit, you will explore the significance of planning within organizations, gaining expertise in strategic planning, tools, and techniques. You will critically assess real-world planning strategies, understanding their strengths and weaknesses. Additionally, you will reflect on the application of planning skills in future personal and professional endeavours, with assessments based on a seminar assignment and a comprehensive test. This Unit will equip you with a solid foundation in planning applicable to various contexts.
Unit 3

Organizing

Continue Learning

In this Unit, you will explore the fundamental concept of organizing within the context of successful organizations. You will delve into the core principles of organizing, its role in resource allocation, coordination, and collaboration, as well as the design of organizational structures. Additionally, you will learn about the impact of technology on organizing and the necessity of adaptability and flexibility within organizational systems. Through a comprehensive assessment, you will demonstrate your understanding of the principles and practices of effective organizing, preparing yourself to structure and manage organizations efficiently.
Unit 4

Leading

Continue Learning

In this Unit, you will explore leadership principles and practices, gaining a comprehensive understanding of the key attributes, skills, and styles that shape leadership in various organizational contexts. You will uncover the foundational principles of leadership, explore a spectrum of leadership styles, understand the pivotal role of leadership within teams, and tackle common leadership challenges. Your ability to apply leadership concepts in real-world contexts will be evaluated through a rigorous assessment, equipping you with the knowledge and skills needed to inspire, guide, and lead individuals and teams toward organizational success, making you a valuable asset in various professional settings.
Unit 5

Controlling and Social Responsibility

Continue Learning

In this comprehensive Unit, you will delve into managers' multifaceted challenges in their professional journeys. You will gain a profound understanding of critical aspects such as human resource management, stress management, conflict resolution in the workplace, change adaptation, resource allocation control, and the ethical dimensions of organizational social responsibility. Through engaging research and essay writing assignments, as well as a rigorous test, you will apply your knowledge and analytical skills to assess, analyze, and propose solutions to real-world managerial challenges, preparing yourself to navigate the complexities of contemporary organizations effectively.
