Types of Managers

Line Managers

Continue Learning

Line managers are responsible for work that directly contributes to the organization's outputs. They play a direct role in the core operations of the organization.

Staff Managers

Continue Learning

Staff managers use special technical expertise to advise and support the efforts of line workers. They provide specialized knowledge to assist in achieving organizational goals.

Functional Managers

Continue Learning

Functional managers have responsibility for a single area of activity within an organization, such as finance, marketing, production, human resources, accounting, or sales.

General Managers

Continue Learning

General managers oversee activities that cover multiple functional areas within an organization. They are responsible for the overall operation of a department or business.

Administrators

Continue Learning

In public or non-profit organizations, managers are often referred to as administrators. Examples include hospital administrators, public administrators, and city administrators.
