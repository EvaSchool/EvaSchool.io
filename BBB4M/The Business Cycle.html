The Business Cycle 

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will have the opportunity to engage in a conversation about the business cycle and its application in the economics of trade. They will also explore the government's involvement in attracting, aiding, and impacting international trade.

Overall Expectations

C2. Assess how political, economic, and geographic factors influence international business methods and operations.

Specific Expectations

C2.1 Evaluate the advantages and disadvantages in both developed countries and developing countries about business opportunities.

C2.2 Explain the impact that geography and political and economic systems have on international business.

C2.3 Describe the roles corporations can play in the setting of international and domestic policy.

Learning Goals

  • Students will understand and apply the business cycle.
  • Students will understand and apply the economics of trade.
  • Students will understand the role the government plays in attracting, assisting, and influencing international trade.
