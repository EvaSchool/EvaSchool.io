Advantages and Disadvantages of International Trade

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will dive into the world of international trade and explore the advantages and disadvantages associated with engaging in global commerce. International trade plays a vital role in modern economies, enabling nations to access a broader market, obtain resources, and stimulate economic growth. 

Overall Expectations

A2. Analyze the impact of international business activity on Canada’s economy.

Specific Expectations

A2.1 Evaluate the benefits and drawbacks of International Trade for Canada.

A2.2 Describe how the state of Canada’s economy affects international businesses operating in Canada.

Learning Goals

  • Students can evaluate the benefits of international trade for Canada.
  • Students will evaluate the drawbacks of international trade to Canada.
