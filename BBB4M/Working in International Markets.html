Working in International Markets

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will be provided with a comprehensive understanding of working in international markets. It explores the challenges and opportunities that arise when operating across borders, equipping students with the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed in diverse global markets. 

Overall Expectations

E3. Demonstrate an understanding of the process for crossing international borders as it relates to international business.

Specific Expectations

E3.1 Explain the role of the Canada Border Services Agency.

E3.2 Identify, drawing on information from a variety of sources, including the Internet, the requirements for study, travel, and employment in different countries.

E3.3 Analyze, drawing on information from a variety of sources, including the Internet, the impact of recent international events.

Learning Goals

  • Students will explain the role of the Canada Border Services Agency.
  • Students will identify, drawing on information from a variety of sources, including the Internet, the requirements for study, travel, and employment in different countries.
  • Students will analyze, drawing on information from a variety of sources, including the Internet, the impact of recent international events.
