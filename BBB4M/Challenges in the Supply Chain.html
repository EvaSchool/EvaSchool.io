Challenges in the Supply Chain 

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will be provided with a deep understanding of the various obstacles and complexities encountered in managing and optimizing supply chain operations. Students will explore the potential challenges that arise at different stages of the supply chain, and gain insights into strategies and best practices to overcome these hurdles.

Overall Expectations

D3. Demonstrate an understanding of the logistics of, and challenges associated with, distribution to local, national, and international markets.

Specific Expectations

D3.1 Compare the logistics of delivering a product to a local, a national, and an international market.


D3.2 Describe the key factors that influence how a company may deliver its product to an international market.


D3.3 Compare the advantages and disadvantages of different modes of transportation for distributing a product to different world markets.


D3.4 Identify, drawing on a variety of sources, including the Internet, information to facilitate the import/export process.

Learning Goals

  • Students will compare the advantages and disadvantages of different modes of transportation for distributing a product to different world markets.
