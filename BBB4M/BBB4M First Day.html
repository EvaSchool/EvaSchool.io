BBB4M – First Day Welcome

BBB4M, International Business Fundamentals

Welcome to BBB4M!

This course is designed to help students improve their reading and writing skills to meet the requirements of the Ontario Secondary School Literacy Graduation Requirement. Throughout the course, students will engage with a wide range of informational, narrative, and graphic texts. They will also develop practical writing skills by producing a variety of forms including summaries, opinions, news reports, and essays.

The course will emphasize reading with understanding, identifying key ideas, and making inferences. Writing instruction will focus on clarity, organization, and effective communication for both academic and real-world contexts. Students will also build strategies for critical thinking, media literacy, and independent learning.

Prerequisite: None, as specified by the Ontario Ministry of Education.

BBB4M is intended to support your growth as a reader, writer, and communicator. Whether you are working toward meeting the Ontario Secondary School Literacy Requirement or simply want to strengthen your literacy skills, this course will provide meaningful opportunities for practice and improvement.

Success in BBB4M depends on your commitment and active participation. Take time to reflect on your learning, seek feedback, and approach each task with a growth mindset. The skills you develop here will serve you well beyond the classroom—in your studies, your career, and your everyday life. Remember that literacy is a journey, and every step forward counts.
