Canada Supply Chain: Assistance

   

Canada’s government has a number of resources available to businesses involved in international trade to assist them with managing their logistics.

  • Canada Border Services Agency
  • Department of Foreign Affairs, Trade and Development
  • The Canadian Trade Commissioner Service
  • Innovation, Science, and Economic Development Canada
  • Export Development Canada
  • Canada Business Network

Helps Canadian businesses grow with confidence by connecting them with our funding and support programs, international opportunities, and the network of trade commissioners in over 160 cities worldwide.
