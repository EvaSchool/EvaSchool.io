Market Research and Marketing Strategies

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will be provided with a comprehensive understanding of the importance of thorough market research and effective marketing strategies in the success of businesses. Students will gain knowledge on how to identify target markets, analyze consumer behavior, and develop strategies to effectively reach and persuade potential customers.

Overall Expectations

D2. Compare the approaches taken by various companies to market their products internationally.

Specific Expectations

D2.1 Identify market-entry strategies used by companies for entering foreign markets.


D2.2 Describe, on the basis of online investigation, the ways in which information and communication technology.


D2.3 Compare marketing approaches taken by companies in different foreign markets.
