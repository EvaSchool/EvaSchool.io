Welcome to Unit One: Kinematics

 

This unit covers the basics of kinematics, focusing on the motion of objects in one and two dimensions. Students will learn concepts like displacement, velocity, and acceleration, as well as how to interpret and create position-time and velocity-time graphs. Through hands-on activities and problem-solving, students will gain practical skills in analyzing and predicting motion. This foundational unit prepares students for more advanced physics and engineering studies.
