Learning Goals

Learn about temperature and thermal energy.

  • Understand basics of kinetic molecular theory to explain phase transition brought about by thermal energy changes.
  • Differentiate between heat and thermal energy
  • Extend the ideas of heat to conservation of energy.
  • Explore temperature change due to heat exchange mechanisms.
  • Explain the phase transition using concepts in heat and energy.
  • Compute the latent heat of fusion or vaporization.
