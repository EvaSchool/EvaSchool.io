Damping and Resonance

DAMPING

a reduction in the amplitude of a wave as a result of energy absorption or destructive interference

RESONANCE

the condition in which the frequency of a wave equals the resonant frequency of the wave’s medium

To ensure that the bell moves and the it produces a loud sound, it must be swung every time. The amplitude of the movement of the bell can reach a maximum when the frequency of the pushing equals the natural frequency or resonant frequency.
