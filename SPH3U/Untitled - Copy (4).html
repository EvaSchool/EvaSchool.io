That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

  • Learn how we define vibrations about an equilibrium point and how the medium of propagation affects the vibrations.
  • Learn about the two fundamental wave types and where they are applied.
  • Learn the properties of a wave that enable us to characterize it and enable us to quantify these properties.
  • Explore the dynamics of waves and see how to determine the speed of a wave in different propagation media.
  • Apply knowledge learned from sound waves (longitudinal waves).
  • Deal with sound intensity as a function of distance from a source.
