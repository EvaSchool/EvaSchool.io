Newton’s First Law of Motion

Lesson Description

Delving into Newton's groundbreaking first law of motion, this section provides a clear understanding of its fundamental principles and applications in dynamic scenarios.

Students will learn how this law serves as a cornerstone for comprehending the behavior of objects at rest or in uniform motion, laying the groundwork for a deeper exploration of the dynamics of the physical world.

Overall Expectations

C3. Demonstrate an understanding of the relationship between changes in velocity and unbalanced forces in one dimension.

Specific Expectations

C3.2 explain how the theories and discoveries of Galileo and Newton advanced knowledge of the effects of forces on the motion of objects

C3.3 states Newton’s laws, and applies them, in qualitative terms, to explain the effect of forces acting on objects.

Learning Goals

  • Learn about Newton’s first law;
  • How to apply Newton’s first law of motion to dynamic situations.

Success Criteria

  • Solve problems involving Newton’s First Law of Motion and apply it to dynamical situations.
