Electric Current

Callout

Direct Current

The electrons flow through a circuit in one direction.

The more freely moving electrons, the greater the direct current.

Measuring Electric Current 

Ammeter: The device used to measure electric current in circuits.

When using this device, it should be placed in series in the circuit.
