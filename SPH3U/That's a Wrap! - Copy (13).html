That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

Understand how force can do work to an object.

Solve work problems.

Learn about Kinetic and Potential energy.

Explore the connection between work and kinetic energy.

Extend the idea of types of energy and see how going from one form to another energy is conserved which is most commonly done with kinetic and potential energies.

Identify the sources of energy.

Learn how to efficiently use the sources of energy.

Learn what power is.

Learn how power impacts us when applied to electrical usage.

Success Criteria

Solve problems about work.

Identify the different types of energy.

Learn how conservation of energy works.

Identify the sources of energy.

Learn how to efficiently use the sources of energy.

Learn about what power is.

Learn how power impacts us when applied to electrical usage.
