Gizmos: An Interactive Learning Adventure!

To learn about different ways to analyze the graphs, do this guided activity on your Gizmo.

If you have not set up your Gizmo account:

  • Go to https://www.explorelearning.com/
  • Set up an account with your kanata email.
  • Your teacher will share with you a code for the class.
  • Do the Gizmo

The gizmo is “Position and velocity graphs.”

  • You will be given a worksheet to complete.
