Chapter 2: Motion in Two Dimensions

This chapter provides a comprehensive exploration of motion in two dimensions, divided into three sections. It begins with a focus on understanding vectors through scale diagrams and then transitions to an algebraic approach for vector manipulation. The final section delves into the intricacies of projectile motion, emphasising the graphical representation and the calculation of displacement and velocity using relevant equations. Students will gain a solid foundation in handling complex two-dimensional motion, equipping them with practical skills for real-world applications.
