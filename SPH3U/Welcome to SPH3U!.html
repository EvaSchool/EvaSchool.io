Unit 1:  Kinematics Unit 2:  Forces Unit 3:  Energy in Society Unit 4:  Waves and Sounds Unit 5: Electricity and Magnetism 

Unleashing Motion's Mysteries

In this foundational unit, we'll dive into the art of motion. With a keen focus on linear motion, we'll explore the intricacies of how objects move. You'll gain a deep understanding of the factors influencing velocity, acceleration, and position. Through hands-on experiments and real-world scenarios, you'll become a master at describing, analyzing, and predicting the paths of objects in motion.

Unveiling Nature's Invisible Hands

Prepare to discover the diverse world of forces that govern our physical existence. Through both qualitative and quantitative investigations, you'll delve into the realm of net forces, acceleration, and mass. You'll uncover how unbalanced forces in one dimension drive changes in velocity, shaping the behavior of everything from falling objects to the orbits of planets.

Powering Our World

Explore the transformative nature of energy and its profound impact on our society. This unit delves into different forms of energy and the laws that govern their conversions. We'll examine the practical applications of energy in our daily lives, addressing energy consumption, conservation, and the environmental implications of our choices.

The Symphony of Vibrations

Embark on a sonorous journey into the world of mechanical waves and sound. Investigate the mesmerizing properties of waves, how they propagate, and the principles behind their creation and reception. From the echoes of a concert hall to the secrets of sonar, you'll unlock the mysteries of sound and its profound impact on our world.

Electrifying Discoveries

Get ready for an electrifying experience as we explore the intricate dance of electricity and magnetism. Dive into magnetic fields and electric circuits, solving intriguing problems along the way. You'll uncover the secrets of magnetic properties, the principles of current flow, and the technologies harnessing these concepts to revolutionize our world.
