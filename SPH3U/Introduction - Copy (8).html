Types of forces

Lesson Description

This section introduces the diverse classifications of forces in the realm of physics.

Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of how forces are represented and categorized, providing a solid foundation for further exploration into the dynamics of motion and interactions within the physical world.

Overall Expectations

C3. Demonstrate an understanding of the relationship between changes in velocity and unbalanced forces in one dimension.

Specific Expectations

C3.1 distinguishes between, and provides examples of, different forces (e.g., friction, gravity, normal force), and describes the effect of each type of force on the velocity of an object.

Learning Goals

  • Students will learn about forces and how to classify forces in physics.

Success Criteria

  • Classify forces in physics.
