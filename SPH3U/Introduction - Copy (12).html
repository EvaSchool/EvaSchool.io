Newton's Third Law of Motion

Lesson Description

Exploring the intricate dynamics of Newton's third law of motion, this section elucidates the principle of action and reaction in the context of dynamic forces.

Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of how every action has an equal and opposite reaction, providing them with a strong conceptual framework for analyzing and predicting the behavior of interacting objects within various physical systems.

Overall Expectations

C2. investigate, in qualitative and quantitative terms, net force, acceleration, and mass, and solve related problems.

Specific Expectations

C2.2 conducts an inquiry that applies Newton’s laws to analyze, in qualitative and quantitative terms, the forces acting on an object and uses free-body diagrams to determine the net force and the acceleration of the object.

Learning Goals

  • Understand Newton’s Third Law of Motion.
  • Apply Newton’s Third Law of Motion to dynamic situations.

Success Criteria

  • Understand Newton’s Third Law of Motion and its application to dynamic situations.
