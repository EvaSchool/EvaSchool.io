Electrical Resistance

Callout

Electrical resistance describes how difficult it is for electric current to travel through a material.

Resistors are electrical devices that has a specific resistance value.

Ohm's Law

The voltage in a conductor is proportional to the current if the temperature remains constant.

 

Callout

Example

What is the resistance of a car starter with 450 A of current and a voltage of 12 V?

  

 Calculate the resistance of a load with a voltage of 30 V and a current of 412 mA.

Callout

Measuring Electrical Resistance

Ohmmeter: The device used to measure electrical resistance in circuits.

When using this device, it should be placed parallel to the circuit and must never be used when the power is on.
