Using Newton's Law

Lesson Description

In this lesson, participants will seamlessly integrate their knowledge of kinematics with Newton's Laws to solve intricate physics problems.

This session focuses on advanced problem-solving techniques, emphasizing critical thinking and analysis of real-world scenarios. By honing these skills, participants will be able to address complex situations, such as predicting motion and understanding forces in practical applications.

Through collaborative problem-solving exercises, individuals will enhance their teamwork and communication skills, leaving the lesson with a heightened proficiency in using Newton's Laws to navigate physics challenges.

Overall Expectations

C2. investigate, in qualitative and quantitative terms, net force, acceleration, and mass, and solve related problems.

Specific Expectations

C2.1 use appropriate terminology related to forces, including, but not limited to mass, time, speed, velocity, acceleration, friction, gravity, normal force, and free-body diagrams [C].

Learning Goals

  • Combine knowledge of kinematics and Newton’s Laws to solve complex situations.

Success Criteria

  • Combine knowledge of kinematics and Newton’s Laws to solve complex situations.
