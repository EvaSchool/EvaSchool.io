Motion in Two-Dimensions: An Algebraic Approach

Lesson Description

This section introduces the concept of working with vectors in two dimensions, emphasising using scale diagrams to represent them graphically. Students will develop a solid understanding of vector manipulation in a two-dimensional space, enabling them to visualise and analyse complex physical scenarios effectively. By the end of this section, students will be proficient in utilizing scale diagrams to comprehend the dynamics of motion in two dimensions.

Overall Expectations

B2. investigate, in qualitative and quantitative terms, uniform and non-uniform linear motion, and solve related problems.

Specific Expectations

B2.7 solve problems involving uniform and non-uniform linear motion in one and two dimensions, using graphical analysis and algebraic equations [AI, C]. 

Learning Goals

  1. Understanding how to work with vectors in two dimensions.
  2. Work with vectors in two dimensions algebraically.

Success Criteria

Work with vectors in two dimensions algebraically.
