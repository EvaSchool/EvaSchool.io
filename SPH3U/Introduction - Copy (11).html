Newton's Second Law of Motion

Lesson Description

This section focuses on Newton's second law of motion and its crucial role in understanding the relationship between force, mass, and acceleration.

Students will grasp the practical applications of this law in dynamic scenarios, enabling them to analyse and predict the behavior of objects under the influence of external forces, thereby solidifying their understanding of the fundamental principles of motion.

Overall Expectations

C2. investigate, in qualitative and quantitative terms, net force, acceleration, and mass, and solve related problems.

Specific Expectations

C2.2 conducts an inquiry that applies Newton’s laws to analyze, in qualitative and quantitative terms, the forces acting on an object, and use free-body diagrams to determine the net force and the acceleration of the object.

Learning Goals

  • Understand Newton’s Second Law of Motion.
  • Apply Newton’s Second Law of Motion to dynamic situations.

Success Criteria

  • Understand Newton’s Second Law of Motion and its application to dynamic situations.
