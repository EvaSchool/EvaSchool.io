Introducing Digestion

Overall Expectations

E.3 Demonstrate an understanding of animal anatomy and physiology and describe disorders of the respiratory, circulatory, and digestive systems.

Specific Expectations

E.3.2 Explain the anatomy of the digestive system and the importance of digestion in providing nutrients needed for energy and growth.

Learning Goals

  1. To define Digestive system.
  2. To understand the four major functions of human digestive system: ingestion, digestion, absorption, and egestion.
  3. To determine the basic functions of parts involved in the system.

Success Criteria

By the end of this lesson, I can…

  1. Define the Digestive system.
  2. Understand the four major functions of the human digestive system: ingestion, digestion, absorption, and egestion.
  3. Determine the basic functions of parts involved in the system.
