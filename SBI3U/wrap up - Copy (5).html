Wrap up

key points

Biogeography provides compelling evidence that remote islands are populated by species that evolved from species that had travelled from the closest major land mass.

• Patterns in embryonic development provide evidence of the evolutionary relationships between species.
• The structures of numerous anatomical features provide strong evidence that they have evolved from an original, homologous structure.
• Vestigial features are rudimentary structures that once performed important functions in ancestral species.
• All species produce more offspring than can survive to reproduce. This results in competition for resources between members of the same species.
