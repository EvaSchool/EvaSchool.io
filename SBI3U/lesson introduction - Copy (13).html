Sexual Reproduction in Seed Plants

Overall Expectations

By the end of this course, students will: 

F2. investigate the structures and functions of plant tissues, and factors affecting plant growth.

Specific Expectations

F3.3 explains the reproductive mechanisms of plants in natural reproduction and artificial propagation (e.g., germination of seeds, leaf cuttings, grafting of branches onto a host tree).&nbsp;

Learning Goals

  • Identify the seed structure and function.
  • Explain the costs and benefits of asexual reproduction.
  • Describe sexual reproduction in Gymnosperms and Angiosperms.
  • Explain Pollination and Fertilization.
