Imagining: Looking Through the Body

Overall Expectations

E.1 Analyze the relationships between changing societal needs, technological advances, and our understanding of the internal systems of humans.

Specific Expectations

E.1.1 Evaluate the importance of various technologies, including Canadian contributions, to our understanding of internal body systems.

Learning Goals

  • Differentiate Invasive from Non-invasive tests.
  • Explain the following imaging technologies used in the diagnosis of diseases:
    • Cardiac Catheterization
    • CT and PET Scans
    • Cardiac Calcium Score
    • Cardiac MRI Scan
  • To explain how insulin works
  • To describe diabetes as well as type 1 and type 2 diabetes
  • To understand the utilization of technology in Insulin delivery
  • To generate a technical report focusing on technology updates that summarizes the available and upcoming technology for the Management of Diabetes

Success Criteria

By the end of this lesson, I can…

Explain how insulin works Describe diabetes as well as type 1 and type 2 diabetes Describe the utilization of technology in Insulin delivery Make a technical report focusing on technology updates that summarize the available and upcoming technology for the Management of Diabetes.
