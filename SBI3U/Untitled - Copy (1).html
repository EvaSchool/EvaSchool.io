Warm-up

Which of the following best describes biodiversity?

A) The study of rocks and minerals in an ecosystem.

 B) The variety of life forms, such as species, genes, and ecosystems, within a given area.

 C) The measurement of the atmospheric conditions in a region.

 D) The study of astronomy and celestial bodies in the universe.

Show Answer

B) The variety of life forms, such as species, genes, and ecosystems, within a given area.
