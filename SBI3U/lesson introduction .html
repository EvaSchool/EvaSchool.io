Nausea, Vomiting, and Diarrhea

Overall Expectations

E.3 Demonstrate an understanding of animal anatomy and physiology and describe disorders of the respiratory, circulatory, and digestive systems.

Specific Expectations

E.3.4 Describe some disorders related to respiratory, digestive, and circulatory systems.

Learning Goals

  1. Differentiate Nausea, Vomiting, and Diarrhea from each other.
  2. Identify some causes and risk factors associated with these conditions.
  3. Suggest possible treatments to prevent/ cure these conditions.

Success Criteria

By the end of this lesson, I can…

  1. Differentiate Nausea, Vomiting, and Diarrhea from each other.
  2. Identify some causes and risk factors associated with these conditions.
  3. Suggest possible treatments to prevent/ cure these conditions.
