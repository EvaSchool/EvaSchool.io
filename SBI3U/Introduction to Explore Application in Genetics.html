Explore Application in Genetics

Overall Expectations

By the end of this course, students will: 

D1. evaluate the importance of some recent contributions to our knowledge of genetic processes and analyse social and ethical implications of genetic and genomic research.

Specific Expectations

D1.1 analyse, on the basis of research, some of the social and ethical implications of research in genetics and genomics (e.g., genetic screening, gene therapy, in vitro fertilization) [IP, PR, AI, C]. 

Learning Goals

Research and assess some of the very latest healthcare applications of genetics in Canada and abroad, gain insights into the promises and challenges they offer, and present your findings and recommendations to the lobby group.

Success Criteria

Present genetics in the findings and recommendations of the very latest healthcare applications of Canada and abroad.
