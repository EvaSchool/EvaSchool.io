Biological Change Over Time

Everywhere we look we see change.

  • We observe life changing overtime; individuals grow and mature and new generations come and go.


The physical Earth also changes. Some changes are rapid, while others are slow and may go unnoticed.

Snowshoe hares have evolved

a number of adaptations that allow them

to survive in winter.

As you learned in the Diversity of Living Things unit, biologists are convinced that

entire species change over time—they evolve.
