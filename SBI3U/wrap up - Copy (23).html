Wrap up

The leaf is the primary site of photosynthesis and gas exchange.

  •  Leaves have adaptations for protecting the plant from herbivores.
  •  Some leaves have specializations for storage or protection against the cold.
  •  Monocots and eudicots have different venation patterns in their leaves.
  •  Humans use plant leaves for food, medicine, and waxes, and in religiousceremonies.
