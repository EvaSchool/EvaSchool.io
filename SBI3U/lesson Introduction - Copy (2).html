Digestion in the Mouth and Stomach

Overall Expectations

E.3 Demonstrate an understanding of animal anatomy and physiology and describe disorders of the respiratory, circulatory, and digestive systems.

Specific Expectations

E.3.2 Explain the anatomy of the digestive system and the importance of digestion in providing nutrients needed for energy and growth.

Learning Goals

  1. Understand the digestive process that takes place in the mouth and stomach.
  •  Physical and Chemical Digestion in the Mouth
  •  Chemical Digestion in the Stomach

2. Understand how foods are digested in the small and large intestines.

3. Explain the structure and functions of the small and large intestines.
