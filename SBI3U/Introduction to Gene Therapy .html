Gene Therapy

Overall Expectations

By the end of this course, students will: 

D1. evaluate the importance of some recent contributions to our knowledge of genetic processes and analyze the social and ethical implications of genetic and genomic research.

Specific Expectations

D1.1 analyze, on the basis of research, some of the social and ethical implications of research in genetics and genomics (e.g., genetic screening, gene therapy, in vitro fertilization) [IP, PR, AI, C].

Learning Goals

Explain the process of gene therapy.

Success Criteria

Elaborate the process of gene therapy.
