The Protists           

     

Overall Expectations

By the end of this course, students will:

B2. Investigate, through laboratory and/or field activities or through simulations, the principles of scientific classification, using appropriate sampling and classification techniques. 

B3. demonstrate an understanding of the diversity of living organisms in terms of the principles of taxonomy and phylogeny.

Specific Expectations

B2.1 uses appropriate terminology related to biodiversity, including, but not limited to: genetic diversity, species diversity, structural diversity, protists, bacteria, fungi, binomial nomenclature, and morphology.

Learning Goals

  • Learn about Protists and its importance
  • Identify the characteristics and phylogeny of protists
  • Learn about the origin of eukaryotes and the life cycle of protists.
  • Describe the interactions of protists in the ecosystem.

Success Criteria

  • Explain protists and their importance.
  • Describe the characteristics and phylogeny of protists.
  • Understand the origin of eukaryotes and the life cycle of protists.
  • Explain the interactions of protists in the ecosystem.
