What have I learned?

 

The lesson on "The Advantages of Being Connected" has provided a comprehensive exploration of the benefits associated with modern connectivity. It has elucidated how advancements in technology have revolutionized communication, access to information, and opportunities for collaboration across various domains of life. Through discussions on the widespread availability of internet access in Canada and the multifaceted benefits it offers, including empowerment through information, cultural exchange, convenience in accessing content and services, and economic opportunities, students have gained a nuanced understanding of the advantages of connectivity. Moreover, the lesson has highlighted how connectivity extends beyond personal benefits to encompass societal advancements in healthcare, education, business, and overall well-being. By examining these interconnected aspects, students are equipped with the knowledge to navigate and harness the benefits of connectivity in their personal, professional, and social lives, contributing to a more interconnected and empowered society.

Learning Goals

  • Acquire knowledge on the foundations of social change.
  • Demonstrate significant technological forces that influence these changes and learn how they  influence Canadian teenagers.
  • Describe the impact of technological changes to society and how Canadians are affected by these changes.
  • Develop research and inquiry skills to interpret, synthesize and analyze research and apply these skills to your findings.
  • Learn how to use social media to advocate for an issue or cause you care about.
