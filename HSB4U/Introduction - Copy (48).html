Unit 5 Reflection

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will reflect on your learning in this unit by answering the reflection questions. 

Overall Expectations

  • B3 Technological Change: demonstrate an understanding of patterns and effects of technological change from a social science perspective.
  • C2 Forces That Shape Social Trends: demonstrate an understanding of how forces influence and shape social patterns and trends.

Specific Expectations

B3.1 Identify some recent technological changes and describe how they affect individuals.

B3.2 Explain how various new technologies (e.g., in medicine, education, entertainment, health and wellness) can affect social structures and interactions .

B3.3 Explain how technological advances lead to cultural adaptations.

C2.1 Describe how trends are shaped by various socio-economic factors.

C2.2 Describe ways in which culture, tradition, and language influence social trends.

C2.3 Explain how increases in population mobility shape social patterns and trends.

C2.4 Explain changes in the nature of work in Canada.

Learning Goals

  • Appreciate the study of change as one of the more significant concepts in social sciences today.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of the major social science forces that influence social change.
  • Describe the schools of thought relating to social change.
