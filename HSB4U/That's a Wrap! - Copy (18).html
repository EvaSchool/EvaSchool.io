What have I learned?

 

In conclusion, the integration of technology into Canadian society has been influenced by various socio-economic factors, including access to resources and economic policies.

Additionally, culture, tradition, and language play pivotal roles in shaping social trends related to technology usage, as they influence societal values and norms surrounding digital engagement. Increases in population mobility have further impacted social patterns and trends, as individuals and communities adapt to changing demographics and migration patterns.

Moreover, changes in the nature of work in Canada, driven by technological advancements, have transformed how technology is incorporated into various industries and professions. As Canadian society navigates these dynamics, it is essential to recognize and address the multifaceted influences that shape technology usage and its implications for individuals and communities.

Learning Goals

  • Appreciate the current trends in Canadian society by looking at different theoretical perspectives.
