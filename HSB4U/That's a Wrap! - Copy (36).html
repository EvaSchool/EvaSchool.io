What have I learned?

 

In conclusion, the lesson on communication has provided a comprehensive exploration of how technology has transformed the way we communicate. By examining recent technological changes, such as the evolution of language in online communication and the impact of spell check and auto-detect software, students have gained insight into the complexities of modern communication. Additionally, the lesson delved into the role of emoticons in bridging communication gaps and the challenges faced by different generations in adapting to evolving communication norms. Furthermore, the lesson highlighted the importance of understanding cultural influences, socio-economic factors, and the evolving nature of work in shaping communication trends. Through this exploration, students have developed a deeper understanding of the dynamic relationship between technology, language, culture, and society, empowering them to navigate and critically evaluate the changing landscape of communication in the digital age.

Learning Goals

  • Acquire knowledge on the foundations of social change.
  • Demonstrate significant technological forces that influence these changes and learn how they  influence Canadian teenagers.
  • Describe the impact of technological changes to society and how Canadians are affected by these changes.
  • Develop research and inquiry skills to interpret, synthesize and analyze research and apply these skills to your findings.
  • Learn how to use social media to advocate for an issue or cause you care about.
