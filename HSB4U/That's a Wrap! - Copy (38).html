What have I learned?

 

The Changing Canadian Landscape lesson explored various aspects of how technology and socio-economic factors are reshaping the Canadian workforce and home environments. We discussed the impact of telecommuting on work productivity, the challenges and benefits of working from home, and the importance of adapting to the evolving workplace. Additionally, we examined the growing value of social media in job searches and workplace expectations among the younger generation. Overall, the lesson highlighted the dynamic shifts occurring in the Canadian landscape due to technological advancements and socio-economic factors, emphasizing the need for individuals to adapt to these changes to thrive in the modern workforce.

Learning Goals

  • Acquire knowledge on the foundations of social change.
  • Demonstrate significant technological forces that influence these changes and learn how they  influence Canadian teenagers.
  • Describe the impact of technological changes to society and how Canadians are affected by these changes.
  • Develop research and inquiry skills to interpret, synthesize and analyze research and apply these skills to your findings.
  • Learn how to use social media to advocate for an issue or cause you care about.
