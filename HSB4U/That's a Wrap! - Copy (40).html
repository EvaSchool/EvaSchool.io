What have I learned?

 

The lesson on Evolving Technology and Unimagined Outcomes explores the multifaceted impact of technological advancements on individuals and society. From the proliferation of virtual social networks to the challenges of techno waste and the inequalities exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic, the lesson delves into the complex interplay between technology and various socio-economic factors. It highlights the transformative power of technology in reshaping social structures, cultural norms, and daily interactions, while also shedding light on the unintended consequences and inequalities that arise. In conclusion, the lesson underscores the need for a balanced and nuanced approach to navigating the evolving landscape of technology, one that acknowledges both its potential for innovation and its capacity to perpetuate social disparities.

Learning Goals

  • Acquire knowledge on the foundations of social change.
  • Demonstrate significant technological forces that influence these changes and learn how they  influence Canadian teenagers.
  • Describe the impact of technological changes to society and how Canadians are affected by these changes.
  • Develop research and inquiry skills to interpret, synthesize and analyze research and apply these skills to your findings.
  • Learn how to use social media to advocate for an issue or cause you care about.
