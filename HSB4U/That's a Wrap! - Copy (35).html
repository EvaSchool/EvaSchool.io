What have I learned?

 

In conclusion, the lesson on the impact of technology has provided a comprehensive understanding of how recent technological advancements affect individuals and society. Through exploring various aspects such as biometrics for identification, the emergence of smart wallets, and the role of social media in communication, students have gained insight into the evolving relationship between technology and social structures. Specifically, they have learned how advances in technology lead to cultural adaptations, such as the transmission of social norms through virtual communities and the potential impact of virtual interactions on real-life social skills. By examining these topics, students have developed a deeper appreciation for the multifaceted ways in which technology shapes contemporary society, highlighting the importance of understanding and critically evaluating the role of technology in our lives.

Learning Goals

  • Acquire knowledge on the foundations of social change.
  • Demonstrate significant technological forces that influence these changes and learn how they  influence Canadian teenagers.
  • Describe the impact of technological changes to society and how Canadians are affected by these changes.
  • Develop research and inquiry skills to interpret, synthesize and analyze research and apply these skills to your findings.
  • Learn how to use social media to advocate for an issue or cause you care about.
