The Impact of Technology

Lesson Description

In this lesson, students will explore the dynamic relationship between technology and society, focusing on recent technological changes and their profound implications on individuals and communities. Through engaging discussions and activities, students will identify and analyze recent technological advancements across various sectors, including medicine, education, entertainment, and health and wellness. They will examine how these technologies shape social structures and interactions, exploring the ways in which innovations like telemedicine, online learning platforms, streaming services, and fitness apps impact daily lives and societal norms. Additionally, students will delve into the concept of cultural adaptation, investigating how technological advances lead to shifts in cultural practices, beliefs, and values. By the end of the lesson, students will gain a comprehensive understanding of the multifaceted impact of technology on individuals, communities, and cultural dynamics, empowering them to critically evaluate the role of technology in shaping contemporary society.

Overall Expectations

B3 Technological Change: demonstrate an understanding of patterns and effects of technological change from a social science perspective.

Specific Expectations

B3.1 Identify some recent technological changes and describe how they affect individuals.

B3.2 Explain how various new technologies (e.g., in medicine, education, entertainment, health and wellness) can affect social structures and interactions .

B3.3 Explain how technological advances lead to cultural adaptations.

Learning Goals

  • Acquire knowledge on the foundations of social change.
  • Demonstrate significant technological forces that influence these changes and learn how they  influence Canadian teenagers.
  • Describe the impact of technological changes to society and how Canadians are affected by these changes.
  • Develop research and inquiry skills to interpret, synthesize and analyze research and apply these skills to your findings.
  • Learn how to use social media to advocate for an issue or cause you care about.
