That's a Wrap!

What have I learned?

In summary, the integration of technology into adolescents' lives has profoundly influenced their behavior and development, offering benefits like improved communication and learning opportunities but also posing challenges such as cyberbullying and digital literacy issues. Understanding this complex relationship is crucial for supporting responsible technology use. Additionally, population trends and demographic concepts, shaped by socio-economic factors, play a significant role in adolescents' access to technology and their digital experiences. Furthermore, cultural influences impact adolescents' attitudes and behaviors towards technology adoption. By considering these factors, we can develop strategies to address the impacts of technology on adolescent development effectively.

Learning Goals

  • Appreciate the changes that happen to an individual and how those changes affect the values and attitudes of society at large.
  • Recognize new trends and challenges adolescents and their families face today.

Success Criteria

You are successful in this lesson if you…

  • Can appreciate the changes that happen to an individual and how those changes affect the values and attitudes of society at large.
  • Can recognize new trends and challenges adolescents and their families face today.
