Literary Devices

Lesson Description

 We will learn about literary devices.

Overall Expectations

B1 Read and demonstrate understanding of a variety of texts for different purposes; 
B2 Use a variety of reading strategies throughout the reading process to extract meaning from texts; 
B4 Locate and extract relevant information from written and graphic texts for a variety of purposes;
C1 write in a variety of forms for different purposes and audiences.

Specific Expectations

B 1.1 read a wide variety of authentic texts of increased complexity on a range of topics;
B 1.2 demonstrate an understanding of complex authentic texts in a variety of ways;
B 1.3 respond to complex authentic texts in a variety of ways;
B 1.4 analyse a variety of texts and explain the relationship between their form and purpose;
B 1.5 analyse texts in a range of genres, including essays, short stories, novels, poems, and drama, to identify literary elements and explain their effect on the reader;
B 2.2 identify different features of texts and explain how they help readers understand the text;
B 2.4 demonstrate an understanding of the grammatical structures of English used in texts appropriate for this level;
B 4.3 compare, synthesize, and evaluate the information gathered from a variety of sources for an independent research project;
C 1.2 write short texts to express ideas and feelings on personal topics using a wide variety of forms.

Learning Goals

  • Demonstrate an understanding of different literary devices.
  • Analyze “The Masque of Red Death” by Edgar Allan Poe to identify literary devices and explain their effect on the reader.
