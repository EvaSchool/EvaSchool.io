Media Literacy

Lesson Description

 We will learn about media literacy.

Overall Expectations

D4 Demonstrate an understanding of, interpret, and create a variety of media texts.

Specific Expectations

D4.1 View, read, and listen to media texts, and identify strategies used in them to influence specific audiences; and 

D4.2 Analyse of current local, national or global issues in a variety of media texts to identify subjective approaches and types of bias;

D4.3 Create a variety of media texts for specific purposes and audiences and explain how the purpose and audience influenced their design decisions and language choice. 

Learning Goals

  • Define Media Literacy;
  • View, read, and listen to various media texts and explain how You influence society.
