Let's Practice!

Use these three distinct phases in the media production process on your own creativity:

  1. Pre-production & storyboarding: idea generation; research; location ideas (interior/exterior); script writing; create a shot list; gather crew, talent (family, friends, & roommates will love being in your video!), equipment, props
  2. Production – shoot everything on your shot list for at least 2-3 minutes, and record all audio needed
  3. Post-production – editing, creating graphics, finalizing
