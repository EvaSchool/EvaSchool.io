That's a Wrap! 

  • The present perfect tenses are used to talk about an action that was completed at an unspecified time or indefinite time in the past.
  • The perfect continuous tenses talked about an action that took place in the past before a specific point of time or another past action.
  • The future perfect tense talks about an action that will occur in the future before a certain point of time or another action in the future.
