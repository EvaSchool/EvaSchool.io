That's a Wrap!

  • Communication tools that deliver information and data 
  • Plural of ‘medium,’ media is an umbrella term for the industries such as TV, Film, Social Media, Video games, and more.
  • Purpose of media: to inform, persuade, describe, and entertain
  • To inform: Provides information to an audience. Often from news programs, like weather reports. 
  • To persuade: It makes a person or group do, buy, or believe something. Used in advertising and political speeches 
  • To describe: Describes an idea, concept, or object. Complements other purposes of media; often not used alone. Posters, books, poems, and more
