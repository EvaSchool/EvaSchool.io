Exploring RACES

Now that you know and have identified the PACTS in the story “The Wedding Present,” we can now answer comprehension questions through RACES.

Callout

The RACE strategy is useful when you are given constructed response questions.

  • Restate the question.
  • Answer the question.
  • Cite evidence from the text.
  • Explain how the evidence connects to the answer.

 

RESTATE

Restate the question. Restating a question is revising that question and turning it into a statement.

Question: What are George and Belinda nervous about?
Answer: George and Belinda are nervous about…

Now restate the following question: 

Do you believe that marriage is a commitment?

ANSWER

After restating the question, answer all the parts completely. 

Question: What are George and Belinda nervous about?
Answer: George and Belinda are nervous about a wedding present inside an envelope that detailed their alternate lives.

Now restate and answer the following question:
 
Do you believe that marriage is a commitment? 

In what way does it show in “The Wedding Present”?

CITE

After answering the question, cite examples and evidence from the story to support your answer.

Question: What are George and Belinda nervous about?

Answer: George and Belinda are nervous about a wedding
present inside an envelope that detailed their alternate lives.

Citing evidence: According to the story, the present they received on their wedding day described that they had a terrible marriage and that they were unhappy with each other.
Do the same for your question! 

Do you believe that marriage is a commitment? In what way does it show in “The Wedding Present”?

EXPLAIN

The last part you have to do is to explain. 

Explain how the evidence connects to the answer.

Question: What are George and Belinda nervous about?

Answer: George and Belinda are nervous about a wedding present inside an envelope that detailed their alternate lives.

Citing evidence: According to the story, the present they received on their wedding day described that they had a terrible marriage and that they were unhappy with each other. After a time, they understood that it truly was a wedding present, saying that it’s the marriage that they aren’t having.
