Story Interpretation

Lesson Description

We will learn about story interpretation through short stories and apply them to reading other texts and answer the homework. 

Overall Expectations

B1 Read and demonstrate understanding of a variety of texts for different purposes;
 
B2 Use a variety of reading strategies throughout the reading process to extract meaning from texts;

B4 Locate and extract relevant information from written and graphic texts for a variety of purposes. 

Specific Expectations

B1.1 read a wide variety of authentic texts of increased complexity on a range of topics;
B1.2 demonstrate an understanding of complex authentic texts in a variety of ways;
B1.3 respond to complex authentic texts in a variety of ways;
B1.4 analyse a variety of texts and explain the relationship between their form and purpose;
B1.5 analyse texts in a range of genres, including essays, short stories, novels, poems, and drama, to identify literary elements and explain their effect on the reader;
B2.2 identify different features of texts and explain how they help readers understand the text;
B2.4 demonstrate an understanding of the grammatical structures of English used in texts appropriate for this level;
B4.3 compare, synthesize, and evaluate the information gathered from a variety of sources for an independent research project.

Learning Goals

  • Demonstrate an understanding of different literary devices.
  • Analyze texts in a range of genres, including essays, short stories, novels, poems, and drama to identify literary devices and explain their effect on the reader.
