Today, we will focus on learning more about Canada’s history and geography. 

Canada is the second biggest country in the world. It is divided into five regions: Atlantic Provinces, Central Canada, Prairie Provinces, West Coast and Northern Territories. 

In this lesson, we will spend some time exploring these regions.

The capital city of British Columbia is Victoria.
