Lesson 3 - Freedom, Rights and Responsibilities of Canadians!

Canada is a nation that values freedom, individual rights, and civic responsibilities. Understanding the dynamics between these elements is essential for academic purposes, as it sheds light on the legal and social framework that shapes Canadian society.

Freedom is a foundational principle of Canadian society, encompassing various aspects such as freedom of expression, religion, and association.

Rights in Canada are enshrined in the Charter of Rights and Freedoms, which forms part of the Constitution Act 1982. 

With rights come responsibilities. Canadian society emphasizes the importance of responsible citizenship.
