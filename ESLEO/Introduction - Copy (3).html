Current Issues in Canada

Lesson Description

In this lesson, we will learn about current issues in Canada.

Overall Expectations

B1 Read and demonstrate understanding of a variety of texts for different purposes. 
B2 Use a variety of reading strategies throughout the reading process to extract meaning from texts.
B4 Locate and extract relevant information from written and graphic texts for a variety of purposes.
C1 Write in a variety of forms for different purposes and audiences
D2 Demonstrate an understanding of the rights and responsibilities of Canadian citizenship, and of the contributions of diverse groups to Canadian society. 
D4 Demonstrate an understanding of, interpret, and create a variety of media texts.

Specific Expectations

B1.1 Read a wide variety of authentic texts of increased complexity on a range of topics. 
B1.2 Demonstrate an understanding of complex authentic texts in a variety of ways.
B2.1 Identify and use the most appropriate reading comprehension strategies before, during, and after reading to understand texts.
B4.1 Locate information from a wide variety of print and electronic sources.
C1.1 Write complex texts to convey information and ideas for academic purposes using a wide variety of forms. 
C1.2 Write short texts to express ideas and feelings on personal topics using a wide variety of forms. 
D2.2 Explain how government policies on equity and social justice apply to current social issues.
D2.3 Use research and presentation skills to inform the class about issues of concern to diverse groups in Canada.
D4.1 View, read, and listen to a variety of media texts, and explain some ways in which they influence society.

Learning Goals

  • Read various news articles to develop your consciousness regarding the current issues in Canada.
  • Demonstrate your understanding of the issues by using the 5Ws + H approach of interpreting news articles
  • Reflect on the current issues in Canada to create advocacy.
