What is PRIVILEGE?

  • The advantages come from being part of a dominant or majority group (White, male, heterosexual, non-disabled, etc.)

Privilege, Bias, Prejudice

  • Racist and discriminatory acts are often at the expense of a disadvantaged group.
  • The power imbalance in society.
  • Your beliefs can make you speak hurtful words and tasteless jokes.
  • Trickles down to societal exclusion.

Why It Is Difficult to Discuss Privilege?

  • Privilege can be seen as ‘normal’ when someone has had it for so long.
  • Look at your own life; what is something that you take for granted?
  • Looking at privilege means looking at our own lives and realizing that our lives are not a 100% reflection of our hard work. 
  • People can feel personally criticized when discussing privilege. Thus, they prefer not to believe in it.
  • Privilege relies on how society works. A big part of privilege is the opportunities that come with it. It can seem small, but it makes a big difference.

Taking Action

  • Privilege cannot be easily eliminated from society.
  • However, you can become more aware of them and change systems of privilege.
  • This is especially true for people in a position of privilege and power!

How to Take Action?

Increase your awareness of privilege.

Participate in the Discussion Forum: Whenever you watch or read anything online, ask yourself what groups are underrepresented. What groups are misrepresented? What groups are making the media you consume?

Do not be silent!
