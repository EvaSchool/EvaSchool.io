That's a Wrap!

  • Adverbs often modify verbs. This means that they describe the way an action is happening. 
  • An adverbial is a word or phrase functioning like an adverb; adverb of manner, adverb of frequency, adverb of time, adverb of place, adverb of certainty, and adverb of degree.
  • Adverbials of manner, time, and some of frequency usually at the end of the clause.
  • Adverbials of certainty, some adverbs of frequency, and time usually go in the middle.
