Understanding Linking Words I

     

Now, we will discuss how to use linking words. This topic is important not only in essays but also in everyday life.

 

Linking Words

Linking words helps you to connect ideas and sentences when you speak or write.

We can use linking words to give examples, add information, summarize, sequence information, give a reason or result, or contrast ideas.

  

   

Examples:

1. My friend is a professor at a medical school. Additionally, She is working as a volunteer to help the poor.

  • The sentence uses the word additionally to share another fact about the person.

2. My friend is a famous actor. In addition, He is incredibly humble.

  • The sentence uses in addition. It is the same reason as above.

3. Computer Games like Call of Duty, are fast-paced first-person shooters.

  • The sentence uses the word like to share an example of a computer game.
