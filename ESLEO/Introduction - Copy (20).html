Media Bias

Lesson Description

 We will learn about the media bias.

Overall Expectations

D4 Demonstrate an understanding of, interpret, and create a variety of media texts.

Specific Expectations

D 4.1 view, read, and listen to a variety of media texts, and explain some ways in which they influence society; and 
D 4.2 Analyse coverage of current local, national, or global issues in a variety of media texts to identify subjective approaches and types of bias.

Learning Goals

  • Understand bias;
  • Understand the reasons why bias might occur in the news;
  • Recognize different ways in which bias can occur in news reporting; and 
  • View, read, listen to various media texts, and write how you influence society.
