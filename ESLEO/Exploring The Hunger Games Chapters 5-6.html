Chapters 5-6

What happened to Katniss at the Remake Center? Consider the meaning and “real world” connections of the following quotes: 

  • “You almost look like a human being now,” and
  • Katniss describes the stylist as “dyed, stencilled, and surgically altered.”

Why do newcomers like Cinna get assigned District 12? In what ways did Cinna differ from most of the other stylists working on the tributes? 

Compare and contrast the hunter/gatherer lifestyle of Katniss in District 12 with that of the people in the Capitol. 

Explain the importance of Katniss getting a sponsor. Why would this be difficult for her? 

Describe the traditional District 12 costumes and how this year is different.

How does the crowd react to the tributes during the Opening Day ceremony? Why did District 12 receive the greatest response from the public? Do you think this has ever happened before with District 12 tributes? Why or why not?

Why is Effie Trinket still with Peeta and Katniss? How is this different from her interactions with the previous District 12?
