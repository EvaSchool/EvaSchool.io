Exploring Everyday English: Scenes at Home

  

English has a lot of applications. One of the very first uses we have for it is in introducing ourselves.

Participate in the Discussion Forum: Where do you live? Describe your house. What rooms are in your house?

  

Does your house have a pool, mini playground, patio or space for you to relax?

  1. What is your favorite room?
  2. Do you have a garden? How would you describe it?
  3. What do you like most about your house?
  4. How many are in the family?
  5. What does your typical day at home look like?
  6. Do you have any roles/ responsibilities in your family?
  7. What memories from your childhood stand out?

Now put all your answers together and organize them to create an essay that talks about your life at home.
