The Hunger Games Chapters 1-4 

Lesson Description

We will read The Hunger Games Chapters 1-4 and interpret and analyze the book. 

Overall Expectations

B1 Read and demonstrate understanding of a variety of texts for different purposes.
B2 Use a variety of reading strategies throughout the reading process to extract meaning from texts.
B3 Use a variety of strategies to build vocabulary.
C1 Write in a variety of forms for different purposes and audiences.
C3 Use correctly the conventions of written English appropriate for this level, including grammar, usage, spelling, and punctuation.

Specific Expectations

B1.1 Read a wide variety of authentic texts of increased complexity on a range of topics. 
B1.2 Demonstrate an understanding of complex authentic texts in a variety of ways
B1.3 respond to complex authentic texts in a variety of ways.
B2.1 Identify and use the most appropriate reading comprehension strategies before, during, and after reading to understand texts.
B3.1 Use a wide variety of vocabulary acquisition strategies to enrich and extend vocabulary.
C1.1 Write complex texts to convey information and ideas for academic purposes using a wide variety of forms.
C3.1 Use correctly the grammatical structures and conventions of written English appropriate for this level.

Learning Goals

  • Read The Hunger Games: Chapters 1-4.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of  The Hunger Games: Chapters 1-4.
  • Respond to The Hunger Games: Chapters 1-4.
