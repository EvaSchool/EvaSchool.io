Lesson 9 - Relative Pronouns: Who, That, Which


In this lesson, you will be introduced and be provided practice with the following relative pronouns: who, that, and which. Relative pronouns are a crucial component of English grammar that helps link two related clauses or pieces of information within a sentence. The three common relative pronouns are "who," "that," and "which." Each of these words serves a specific role in connecting and clarifying the relationships between different parts of a sentence.
