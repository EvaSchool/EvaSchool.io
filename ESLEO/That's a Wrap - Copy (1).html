Writing a Writing a Discussion Essay - Parts

Introduction

  • Presents your topic, provides background and gives your statement.

Body

  • Core arguments and analysis, presents evidence (e.g. quotes)
  • One main point per paragraph
  • Each paragraph starts with a topic sentence
  • All paragraphs relate to your thesis

Conclusion

  • Ties together your main points
  • Shows why your argument matters
