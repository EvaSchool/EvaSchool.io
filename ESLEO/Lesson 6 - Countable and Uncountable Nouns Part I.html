Lesson 6 - Countable and Uncountable Nouns Part I

In this lesson, you will learn countable and uncountable nouns with a/an, some and any. You will use their knowledge of countable and uncountable nouns to talk about food items using a/an, some, and any. In the English language, nouns can be classified into two primary categories: countable nouns and uncountable nouns. These distinctions are essential for proper grammar and communication. Let's explore the characteristics of each type and how they impact sentence structure.
