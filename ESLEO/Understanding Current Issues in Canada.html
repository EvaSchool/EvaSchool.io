Understanding Current Issues in Canada

Participate in the Discussion Forum: How will you define the word “advocacy” now?

Advocacy means taking action to create change. Before we can take action, we need to develop an awareness of our country’s problems today. 

In this lesson, we will learn more about these current issues.
