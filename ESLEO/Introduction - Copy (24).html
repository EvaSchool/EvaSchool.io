Enough and Not Enough Part II

Lesson Description

 We will review enough and not enough. We will be introduced to more than enough. We will be introduced to it and compare its use to more than enough. We will demonstrate too much and too many before nouns and too much as an adverb. We will also practice positive and negative feelings using too, enough, etc.
