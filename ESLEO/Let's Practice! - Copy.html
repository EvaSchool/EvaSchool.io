An advocacy is a coordinated and strategic effort to promote a specific cause, idea, or policy change. It involves mobilizing individuals or groups to raise awareness about an issue, influence public opinion, and ultimately bring about a desired change or outcome.

You can choose one of the various advocacy issues and topics and describe how you will promote this situation.
