Enough and Not Enough Part I

Lesson Description

We will be introduced to enough and not enough with nouns, adjectives, adverbs and verbs. You will be introduced to the structure: enough + noun + something AND enough + noun + for + something. 

Overall Expectations

B1     Read and demonstrate understanding of a variety of texts for different purposes.
C3     Use correctly the conventions of written English appropriate for this level, including grammar, usage, spelling, and punctuation.

Specific Expectations

B1.1 Read a wide variety of authentic texts of increased complexity on a range of topics (e.g., textbook chapters, charts and tables, magazine articles, essays, literary texts from a range of cultures, including Aboriginal cultures: short stories, novels, plays, satire, poetry);
C3.1 Use correctly the grammatical structures and conventions of written English appropriate for this level.

Learning Goals

  • Use correctly the grammatical structures and conventions of written English appropriate for this level.
