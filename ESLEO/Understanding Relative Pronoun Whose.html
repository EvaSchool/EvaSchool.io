Understanding Relative Pronoun: Whose

WHOSE

Whose is a pronoun used in questions to ask who owns something or has something. 

In other words, whose is about possession. 

Whose is followed by a noun (the thing that is possessed). 

I feel bad for the wife. His husband got into an accident. 
I feel bad for the wife whose husband got into an accident. 

Whose refers back to the wife, who possesses the husband. Whose replaces his.
