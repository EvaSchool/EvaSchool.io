International Economic Developments

Lesson Description

In this lesson, we will explore International Economic Developments. We'll investigate how globalization and technological advancements impact the global economy. We'll also examine trade agreements, economic crises, emerging markets, and their effects on international economic relations. This lesson provides insights into the dynamic nature of the global economy and its significant trends.

Overall Expectations

E2 International Economic Developments: analyzes the impact of some key international economic events and developments as well as various responses to them.

Specific Expectations

E2.1 explain how globalization influences the economic decisions of individuals, firms, and governments.

E2.2 explain the significance of a variety of international events/developments (e.g., natural disasters, war, terrorism, changes in governments, technological changes, increasing international debt) and policies (e.g., with respect to trade, the environment, energy, and security) for the Canadian economy.

E2.3 describe the Canadian government's responses to global economic challenges that affect stability.

E2.4 describe ways in which individuals and groups attempt to address problems related to international economic activities.

Learning Goals

  • Students will explain how globalization influences the economic decisions of individuals, firms, and governments.
  • Students will explain the significance of a variety of international events/developments (e.g., natural disasters, war, terrorism, changes in governments, technological changes, increasing international debt) and policies (e.g., with respect to trade, the environment, energy, security) for the Canadian economy.
  • Students will describe Canadian government responses to global economic challenges that affect stability.
  • Students will describe ways in which individuals and groups attempt to address problems related to international economic activities.
