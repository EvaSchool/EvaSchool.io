Supply and Demand Vocabulary 

Demand

The relationship between possible prices and the quantities consumers want to purchase.

Quantity demanded

The amount of a good/service consumers is willing to purchase at a given price.

Law of demand

There is an inverse relationship between price and quantity. The greater the price, the less quantity demanded and vice-versa.

Demand schedule

The table shows various possible prices and quantities demanded.

Demand curve

The graph represents the possible price/quantity demanded.

Supply

The relationship between possible prices and the quantities producers are willing to offer for purchase.

Quantity supplied

The amount of a good/service producer is willing to sell at a given price.

Law of supply

There is a direct relationship between price and quantity.  The greater the price, the more producers are willing to produce, and vice-versa.

Supply schedule

The table shows various possible prices and quantities supplied.

Supply curve

The graph represents the possible price/quantity supplied.
