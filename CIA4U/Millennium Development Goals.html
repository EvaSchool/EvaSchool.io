Millennium Development Goals

Callout

End poverty by 2015. This was the historic promise 189 world leaders made at the United Nations Millennium Summit held in New York in September 2000 when they signed onto the Millennium Declaration and agreed to meet the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) for advancing humanity and the survival of humankind.
