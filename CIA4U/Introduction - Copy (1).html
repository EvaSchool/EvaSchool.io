Supply and Demand

Lesson Description

In this lesson, we will uncover the fundamental economic principles of supply and demand. These concepts drive the dynamics of markets, from individual consumer choices to global economic policies.

Overall Expectations

B2 Supply and Demand Models: demonstrate an understanding of supply and demand models, including how to apply these models, and of factors that affect supply and demand.

Specific Expectations

B2.1 demonstrate an understanding of models of supply and demand, including price elasticity, and apply these models to analyse selected economic decisions 
B2.2 explain how various factors, including taxation, affect supply and demand
B2.3 use supply and demand models to Analyse consumer decision making. 

Learning Goals

  • Students will demonstrate an understanding of models of supply and demand, including price elasticity, and apply these models to analyse selected economic decisions
  • Students will explain how various factors, including taxation, affect supply and demand
  • Students will use supply and demand models to Analyse consumer decision making.
