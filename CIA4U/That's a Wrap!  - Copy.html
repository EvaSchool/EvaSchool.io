In this lesson, we will delve into the fundamental aspects of thinking and decision-making. Thinking encompasses mental processes like problem-solving and creativity, while decision-making involves selecting the best course of action from various options. Understanding these processes equips us with essential tools to make informed choices, tackle problems, and navigate life's challenges. We will explore thinking strategies, decision-making models, and the factors influencing our choices, enhancing our ability to lead intentional and fulfilling lives.
