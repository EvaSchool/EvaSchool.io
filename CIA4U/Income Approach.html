Income Approach

The production of national products or factor incomes from factors of production generates all income.

Rent (land) (r)
Wages (labor) (w/s)
Interest (capital) (i) [includes dividends paid to shareholders]
Profit (capital) ( [if profits are not distributed, called retained earnings] - depreciation [wear and tear on equipment and machinery and indirect taxes are included in profits] in order to balance the expenditure approach).
