Graphing Supply and Demand

Many factors influence price, but market forces generally determine the equilibrium price and quantity. A market is any network that brings buyers and sellers into contact with each other to exchange goods and services.

The point where the two lines intersect (meet) is called equilibrium (where the quantity demanded, and the quantity supplied are equal. This is where the market will settle, provided there is no interference.)
