Unit 1 Summative Task

Learning Goals

  • Complete an overall assessment of your learning for this unit.
  • Communicate your learning in a variety of ways. 

Overall Expectations

B1 Scarcity and Choice: demonstrate an understanding of the significance of the concept of scarcity and how it influences economic choices and decisions of various economic stakeholders.

B4 Economic Thought and Decision Making: analyze how economic and political ideas and various sociocultural factors affect economic decision-making.

Specific Expectations

B1.2 explain, using both normative and positive economic analysis, the interrelationship between needs/wants, production decisions, and consumer choices and decisions.

B1.3 demonstrate an understanding of production possibilities curves (PPCs) and how they can be used to guide economic decisions about the allocation of scarce resources.

B1.4 explain how the concepts of scarcity and opportunity costs influence economic decisions, at both the personal and societal levels.

B4.1 explain the key ideas of a variety of prominent economic theorists.
