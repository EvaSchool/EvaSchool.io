Exchange Rate

The exchange rate is the value of one currency for conversion to another. If you travel to another country and you want to purchase any goods/services, you need to use the currency of the country you are in. Before you go, you would exchange Canadian dollars for the currency of your destination. You can do this at a variety of financial institutions. 

The value of currency changes, compared to another, depending on market forces (supply and demand). If the currency varies depending on the market, it is called a floating rate. Sometimes, countries will not allow the exchange rate to float and choose to have a fixed rate.
