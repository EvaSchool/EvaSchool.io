CIA4U – First Day Welcome

CIA4U, Analysing Current Economic Issues, Grade 12 University Preparation

Welcome to CIA4U!

Most students taking this course will be preparing for university, so think of this course as practice for that next step. Please read instructions carefully, reflect on your learning regularly, and aim to improve with each assignment. Be thoughtful, analytical, and willing to challenge yourself—your effort will be recognized.

The course will emphasize reading with understanding, identifying key ideas, and making inferences. Writing instruction will focus on clarity, organization, and effective communication for both academic and real-world contexts. Students will also build strategies for critical thinking, media literacy, and independent learning.

Prerequisite: Any Grade 11 or 12 university (U) or university/college (M) preparation course in Social Sciences and Humanities, English, or Canadian and World Studies.

CIA4U is intended to support your growth as a reader, writer, and communicator. Whether you are working toward meeting the Ontario Secondary School Literacy Requirement or simply want to strengthen your literacy skills, this course will provide meaningful opportunities for practice and improvement.

Success in CIA4U depends on your commitment and active participation. Take time to reflect on your learning, seek feedback, and approach each task with a growth mindset. The skills you develop here will serve you well beyond the classroom—in your studies, your career, and your everyday life. Remember that literacy is a journey, and every step forward counts.
