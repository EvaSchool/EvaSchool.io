Unit 4 Summative Task

Overall Expectations

Theories and Models of International Trade: analyze various theories, models, and issues relating to international trade

International Economic Developments: analyze the impact of some key international economic events and developments as well as various responses to them

International Economic Power and Inequality: explain the main causes and effects of global economic disparities and assess the effectiveness of responses to these disparities 

Specific Expectations

E1.1 demonstrate an understanding of key aspects of trade theories.

E1.2 demonstrate an understanding of exchange rate regimes (i.e., fixed, managed, and floating exchange rates) and how they influence the terms of trade.

E1.3 assess trade models and practices (e.g., fair trade, free trade agreements, trade barriers, marketing boards) with reference to both economic and ethical criteria.

E1.4 analyze the objectives and influence of a variety of trade agreements and political/ economic organizations that focus on trade.

E2.1 explain how globalization influences the economic decisions of individuals, firms, and governments.

E2.2 explain the significance of a variety of international events/developments (e.g., natural disasters, war, terrorism, changes in governments, technological changes, increasing international debt) and policies (e.g., with respect to trade, the environment, energy, and security) for the Canadian economy.

E2.3 describe the Canadian government's responses to global economic challenges that affect stability.

E2.4 describe ways in which individuals and groups attempt to address problems related to international economic activities.

E3.1 analyze data on global economic disparities and explain the main causes and effects of economic marginalization.

E3.2 assess responses to economic disparity by various intergovernmental organizations. 

E3.3 explain how various social movements and social justice organizations address global economic inequality, and assess their effectiveness.

Learning Goals

  • Complete an overall assessment of your learning for this unit. 
  • Communicate your learning in a variety of ways.
