Underground Economy

There are other business transactions that we only sometimes think about. These include what is known as the underground economy. 

According to the Canada Revenue Agency, the underground economy is any unreported or under-reported activity for tax and GST/HST purposes. 

Often called "moonlighting" or "working under the table," it can include bartering, failing to file tax returns, omitting an entire business activity from your tax return, "skimming" a portion of business income from what you report on your taxes, and not reporting a portion of employment income like tips and gratuities.
