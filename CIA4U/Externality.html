Externality

 An positive or negative impact on any party not involved in a given economic transaction or act.


A positive externality (also called "external benefit," "external economy," or "beneficial externality") is the positive effect an activity imposes on an unrelated third party.


Similar to a negative externality, it can arise either on the production or consumption sides.
