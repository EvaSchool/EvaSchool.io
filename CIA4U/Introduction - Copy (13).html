International Economic Power and Inequality

Lesson Description

In this lesson, we will delve deep into the intricate world of International Economic Power and Inequality. This topic takes us on a journey through the dynamics of global economic relations, exploring the distribution of power and resources among nations, as well as the disparities in wealth and income that exist on both national and international scales.

Overall Expectations

E3 International Economic Power and Inequality: explain the main causes and effects of global economic disparities and assess the effectiveness of responses to these disparities. 

Specific Expectations

E3.1 analyze data on global economic disparities and explains the main causes and effects of economic marginalization.

E3.2 assess responses to economic disparity by various intergovernmental organizations.

E3.3 explain how various social movements and social justice organizations address global economic inequality and assess their effectiveness.

Learning Goals

  • Students will analyze data on global economic disparities and explain the main causes and effects of economic marginalization
  • Students will  assess responses to economic disparity by various intergovernmental organizations
  • Students will explain how various social movements and social justice organizations address global economic inequality and assess their effectiveness.
