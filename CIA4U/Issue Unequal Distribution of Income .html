Issue: Unequal Distribution of Income 

One of the significant issues that our economy faces, as well as many countries in the world, is unequal income distribution. You will learn more about this in Unit 3, but for this unit, you will look at some evidence, the causes and effects, and possible solutions to determine what actions could be taken.
