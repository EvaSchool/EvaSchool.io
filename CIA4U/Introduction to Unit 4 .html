Welcome to Unit 4 

In this unit, we'll explore key aspects of international economics. We'll start with Theories and Models of Trade, examining why countries trade and the benefits they gain. Then, we'll dive into International Economic Developments to understand the evolving global economic landscape. We'll also explore the dynamics of International Economic Power, focusing on influential global actors. Lastly, we'll investigate Inequality in the international economic sphere, both among nations and within them. This unit will provide a comprehensive view of the complexities of international economics.
