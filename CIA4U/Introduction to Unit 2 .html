Unit 2 Introduction 

In this unit, we embark on a journey through the economic landscape, where we'll explore the dynamics of firms and market structures, delve into the art of economic decision-making and trade-offs, and examine the vital role of government in addressing imbalances and fostering fairness in our economic system. We'll gain insights into how businesses operate within different market structures, uncover the essence of economic choices and trade-offs, and understand how government policies can correct market inefficiencies. This unit equips us to navigate the intricacies of economics, from individual decision-making to broader economic policies that shape our world.
