Welcome to Unit 3!

In this unit, we'll explore Macroeconomic Measures and Models, Fiscal Policy, and Monetary Policy. We'll start by understanding key economic indicators and models. Then, we'll delve into how governments use fiscal policy to manage their finances and influence the economy. Finally, we'll look at how central banks use monetary policy tools to control the money supply and promote economic stability. This unit will provide essential insights into the macroeconomic forces that shape economies and policies.
