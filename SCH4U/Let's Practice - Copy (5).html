Q1. A sample of tap water has a measured pH value of 6.8 at 25 °C. What is its pOH? 

Show Answer

Answer: 

pH + pOH = 14

         pOH = 14.00 – pH

          = 14.00 – 6.8

         pOH = 7.2   

Q2, What color does Phenolphthalein show in acid mixtures? In base?

Show Answer

Answer: Phenolphthalein is colorless in acidic solutions and turns pink in basic solutions. This is because the molecule undergoes a structural change when the pH changes, which affects the way it absorbs and emits light, resulting in a visible color change around a pH of 8.2 to 10.
