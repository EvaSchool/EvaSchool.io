Q.1  Identify the entities that are oxidized and reduced in this reaction.

(a) Cu(s) + 2 Ag+ (aq) →2 Ag(s) + Cu2 + (aq)

Show Answer

Solution:

  • The entity oxidized is Cu(s), as it loses electrons to become Cu2+.
  • The entity reduced is Ag+, as it gains electrons to become Ag(s).
