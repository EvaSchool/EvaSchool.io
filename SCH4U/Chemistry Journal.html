The Impetus for Innovation

International relations between the United States and Japan deteriorated and silk became scarce in the US. As this happened, DuPont focused on synthetic fibers.

   

Carothers switched his focus from rubbers to polyamides and reviewed 81 polyamides. He mainly focused on “the 6,6 polymer” because its monomers were hexane-1,6-diamine (hexamethylene diamine) and hexanedioic acid (adipic acid).
