Welcome to Unit 3: Energy Changes and Rates of Reaction

Learning Goals

In this lesson you should achieve the following learning goals:

  • Recognize that rates of chemical reactions vary, and identify factors that affect the rate of a reaction
  • Describe how the rate of disappearance of a reactant and appearance of a product are stoichiometrically related
  • Explain how the rate of a reaction is determined by the series of elementary steps that make up the reaction
