Please review the following Learning Goals of this section and check the Success Criteria you already achieved.

Learning Goals

Students can identify oxidation numbers and how they are assigned in chemical reactions.

Students can determine whether the substances are oxidizing or reducing agents.

Students can understand the oxidation numbers method.

Students can learn the half reactions method.

Students can understand and analyze how to predict redox reactions using redox table.

Success Criteria

Student's can… 

  1. Write oxidation numbers and assign them in chemical reactions.
  2. Identify the oxidizing and reducing agents.
  3. Explain and analyze how to predict redox reactions using redox table.
