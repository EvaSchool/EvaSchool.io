Learning Goals

Students will Learn about atoms

Students will Know the development of atomic theory

Students will Learn about isotopes

Students will Know about quantum theory

Students will Understand Bohr’s atomic model

Students will Know about spectroscopy

Students will Learn about the quantum numbers

Students will Explain the significance of each quantum number.

Students will Understand the arrangement of elements in periodic table

Students will Know aufbau principle

Students will Write electron configurations of elements

Students will Understand ions and magnetism.

Students will Know about applications of quantum mechanics.

Success Criteria

Student's can...

  1. Explain atoms
  2. Explain the development of atomic theory
  3. Explain isotopes
  4. Explain quantum theory
  5. Explain Bohr’s atomic model
  6. Understand spectroscopy
  7. Know about Schrodinger’s Theory
  8. Learn about quantum mechanical model of an atom
  9. Understand Heisenberg’s Uncertainty Principle
  10. Understand the arrangement of elements in periodic table.
  11. Know aufbau principle.
  12. Write electron configurations of elements.
  13. Understand ions and magnetism.
