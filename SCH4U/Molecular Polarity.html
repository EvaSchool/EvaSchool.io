Molecular Polarity

 

Callout

Polar molecule

  • a molecule that has a net dipole. 
  • water is a polar molecule with a net negative dipole in the direction of the oxygen atom. 

Nonpolar molecule

  • has a bond dipole sum of zero
  • for example, a carbon dioxide molecule with a negative charge distribution on the oxygen atom and a positive charge distribution on the central carbon atom have polarities that are equal and opposite, canceling each other out.
