POLYPROTIC ACIDS

MONOPROTIC ACID - - an acid that possesses ONLY ONE ionizable (acidic) hydrogen atom

POLYPROTIC ACID - - an acid that possesses MORE THAN ONE ionizable (acidic) hydrogen atom

Sulfuric acid has 2 hydrogen atoms (represented as white spheres) that may be removed, one at a time, when the acid ionizes.
