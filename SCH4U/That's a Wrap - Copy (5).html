Learning Goals

Students can understand the characteristics of Galvanic cells.

Students can describe galvanic cells in terms of oxidation and reduction half-cells whose voltages can be used to determine overall cell potential.

Students can understanding corrosion of metals in terms of an electrochemical process, and describing some common corrosion-inhibiting techniques.

Students can understanding Electrolysis.

Students can describing electrolytic cells and their relationship to galvanic cells.

Students can understanding Electrolysis of water and commercial electrolytic processes.

Students can understanding Cold Fusion: The Discovery that Never Happened.

                    

Success Criteria
