Electronegativity and Bond Polarity

 

Callout

Bond Polarity

  • is the separation of electric charge along the bond.
  • affects the chemical properties of molecules.

Electronegativity

  • the ability of an atom in a molecule to attract shared electrons to itself; atoms of different elements have different electronegativities
  • Linus Pauling created a method for determining the electronegativity values of atoms
  • and the trend in the periodic table: increases from left to right and decreases going down in a group in the periodic table of elements. 

Bond Polarities and Dipoles in Diatomic Molecules

 Dipole  - a separation of positive and negative charges in a region of space
