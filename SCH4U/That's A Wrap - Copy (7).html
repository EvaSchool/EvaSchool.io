Learning Goals

Student's will be able to:

Discuss Arrhenius’ theory of acids and bases.

Discuss Brønsted-Lowry’s theory of acids and bases.

Identify conjugate acid-base pairs.

Write acid ionization constant equations.

Discuss equilibrium in an industrial process.

Solve problems involving solutions of strong bases.

Understand the issues in acids and bases and how they affect consumers’ decision in buying products with chemical contents.

Success Criteria

Student's can …

Explain Arrhenius theory of acids and bases.

Explain Brønsted-Lowry theory of acids and bases.

Determine conjugate acid-base pairs.

Write acid ionization constant equations.

Solve problems involving solutions of strong bases.

Solve problems involving solutions of weak bases.

Explain Acid-Base Titration and determine the titrant concentrations.

Illustrate how acid-base titrations are used to analyze various characteristics of acids and bases.

Understand titrations Involving weak acids and strong bases.
