That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

Students will be able to: 

  1. Review integer exponent rules.
  2. Simplify expressions involving integer exponents.
  3. Review integer exponent rules.
  4. Simplify expressions involving integer exponents.
  5. Manipulate the parent exponential function graphically.
  6. Apply transformations to the equations of exponential functions.
  7. Solve real-world problems involving exponential growth and decay.
  8. Use graphs and equations to solve problems.

Success Criteria

Students will know that you are successful if you can:

  1. Use exponent rules to simplify exponents.
  2. Recognize patterns that model exponential growth.
  3. Simplify rational expressions.
  4. Explore ways to simplify expression with rational exponents.
  5. Apply transformations to the equations of exponential functions to create a new graph.
  6. Use graphs and equations to solve exponential problems.
  7. Use graphs and equations to solve real-world problems involving exponential growth and decay.
