That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

In this lesson students will be able to:

  1. Explore linear patterns.
  2. Develop a formula for arithmetic sequences.
  3. Explore non-linear patterns.
  4. Determine a formula for geometric sequences.
  5. Explore the sum of arithmetic series.
  6. Determine an equation for arithmetic series.
  7. Explore the sum of geometric series.
  8. Determine the equation for calculating the sum of a geometric series.
  9. Connect Pascal’s Triangle to series.
  10. Use Pascal’s Triangle to quickly solve binomial expansion.

Success Criteria

  • Students will be successful if:
  • Develop a formula for arithmetic sequences.
  • Determine a formula for geometric sequences.
  • Determine ways to solve recursive sequences.
  • Calculate the sum of the terms of a geometric sequence.
  • Use Pascal’s Triangle to quickly solve binomial expansion.
