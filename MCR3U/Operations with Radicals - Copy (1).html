Operations with Radicals

Key Idea

Entire radicals can sometimes be simplified by expressing them as the product of two radicals, one of which contains a perfect square. This results in a mixed radical.

Need to Know

The only radicals that can be added or subtracted into a single term are radicals.

An answer containing a radical is an exact answer. An answer containing a decimal is an approximate answer.

 A mixed radical is in simplest form when the smallest possible number is written under the radical sign.
