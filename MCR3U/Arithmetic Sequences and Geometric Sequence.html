What is a sequence and a term?

Click learn more to find out!

A sequence is a list of numbers that follow a specific pattern or rule. Each number in the sequence is called a term.

Accordion

What we need to know?

Arithmetic Sequence

It's a sequence that has the same difference, the common difference, between any pair of consecutive terms.



Recursive Sequence

It's a sequence for which one term (or more) is given and each successive term is determined from the previous term(s).

General term

It's a formula, labelled tn that expresses each term of a sequence as a function of its position. For example, if the general term is tn = 2n, then to calculate the 12th term (t12), substitute.



Recursive Formula

It is a formula relating the general term of a sequence to the previous term(s).
