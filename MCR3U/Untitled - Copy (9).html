The Fish Hatchery Exploration

At a fish hatchery, fish hatch at different times even though the eggs were all fertilized at the same time. The number of fish that hatched on each of the first four days after fertilization was 2, 10, 50, and 250, respectively. If the pattern continues, calculate the total number of fish hatched during the first 10 days.

Show Answer

The total number of fish that hatched during the first 10 days at the fish hatchery, following the identified pattern, is 4,882,812.
