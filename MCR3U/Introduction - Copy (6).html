Learning Goals

Specific Expectations

Learning Goals

Overall Expectations

C3. make connections between sequences, series, and financial applications, and solve problems involving compound interest and ordinary annuities.

Specific Expectations

C3.5 explains the meaning of the term annuity and determines the relationships between ordinary simple annuities (i.e., annuities in which payments are made at the end of each period, and compounding and payment periods are the same), geometric series, and exponential growth, through the investigation with technology (e.g., use a spreadsheet to determine and graph the future value of an ordinary simple annuity for varying numbers of compounding periods; investigate how the contributions of each payment to the future value of an ordinary simple annuity are related to the terms of a geometric series)
