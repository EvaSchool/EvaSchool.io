Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will complete an assessment of your learning for this chapter that is provided by your teacher. To complete this lesson you should:

  1. Notify your teacher that you are ready to complete your chapter assessment of learning.They will share a project or test for you to complete. 
  2. Complete the assessment of learning given to you by your teacher in the agreed upon timeframe.
  3. Submit your completed work to your teacher by putting a copy of your work in your course folder.
