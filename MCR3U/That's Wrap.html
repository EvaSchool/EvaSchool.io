That's a Wrap!

Please review the following Learning Goals of this section and check the Success Criteria you already achieved.

Learning Goals

In this lesson, student will:

  • Review quadratic relations and their graphs.
  • Identify the vertex and axis of symmetry in quadratic graphs.
  • Determine the highest and lowest points of quadratic functions.
  • Identify an algorithm to find the vertex of a quadratic.
  • Solve quadratic equations in multiple ways.
  • Use strategies like factoring, completing the square, and using the quadratic formula to solve quadratics.
  • Solve problems involving the intersection of a linear and a quadratic function.

Success Criteria

You will know that you are successful if you can:

  • Recognize a quadratic function from its graph and/or equation
  • Identify key points of a quadratic function, such as the vertex and axis of symmetry
  • Explain what the inverse of a function is.
  • Graph the inverse of quadratic functions.
  • Use substitution to determine the points of intersection algebraically
  • Classify quadratics into different categories.
  • se characteristics to help you classify quadratics.
