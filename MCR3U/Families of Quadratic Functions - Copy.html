Need to Know

  • The algebraic model of a quadratic function can be determined algebraically.
  • If the zeros are known, write in factored form with an unknown, substitute another known point, and solve for a.
  • If the vertex is known, write in vertex form with an unknown, substitute a known point, and solve for a.
