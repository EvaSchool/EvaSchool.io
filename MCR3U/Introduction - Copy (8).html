Determining Maximum and Minimum Values of a Quadratic Function

Lesson Description

I can find the maxima of quadratic functions.
I can determine the vertex of a quadratic equation using an algorithm.

Overall Expectations

A2. Determine the zeros and the maximum or minimum of a quadratic function, and solve problems involving quadratic functions, including problems arising from real-world applications.

Specific Expectations

A2.2 Determine the maximum or minimum value of a quadratic function whose equation is given in the form f(x) = ax2 + bx + c, using an algebraic method (e.g., completing the square; factoring to determine the zeros and averaging the zeros).

Learning Goals

  • Students will determine the highest and lowest points of quadratic functions.
  • Students will identify an algorithm to find the vertex of a quadratic.
