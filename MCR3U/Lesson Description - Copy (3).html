Lesson Description

In this lesson you will learn how to identify transformations of the basic functions and describe them in proper mathematical language. You will also learn how to apply transformations to the equation of the parent function. To complete this lesson you should:

  1. Make a copy of the following template to and use it keep notes for this lesson to review before tests and your exam: Student Note
  2. Ask your teacher for the Pear Deck link to this lesson. Go through the Pear Deck and make sure to respond to all of the student prompts. Notify your teacher once you have finished it.
