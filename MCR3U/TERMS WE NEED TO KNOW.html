Terms We Need To know:

Timeline

  • 1. SIMPLE INTEREST

    Interest earned or paid only on the original sum of money that was invested or borrowed. 

  • 2. PRINCIPAL

    a sum of money that is borrowed or invested.

  • 3. INTEREST

    the money earned from an investment or the cost of borrowing money.

  • 4. AMOUNT

    The total value of an investment or loan. The amount is given by A = P + I where A is the amount, P is the principal, and I is the interest.

  • 5. Simple interest

    Can be calculated using the formula: 

    I = Prt

    where:

    I = interest

    P = principal amount

    r = interest rate, expressed as a decimal

    t = time, expressed in the same period as the interest rate, usually per year

  • 6. Total amount, A,

    of an investment earning simple interest can be calculated using the formula:

    A = P( 1 +rt) or A = P + Prt

    where:

    A = total amount of investment

    P = principal amount

    r = interest rate, expressed as a decimal

    t = time, expressed in the same period as the interest rate, usually per year
