Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will reflect on your learning in this chapter by answering a discussion forum that your teacher will assign you. To complete this lesson please do the following:

  1. Please complete the discussion post titled, “Chapter 1 Looking Back”.
  2. Comment on at least one other student’s response.
  3. Make a copy of the following document and fill it in with your comfort level of each learning goal: Chapter 1 Learning Goals
