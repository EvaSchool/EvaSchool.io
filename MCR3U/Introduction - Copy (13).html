Linear-Quadratic Systems 

Lesson Description

I can use substitution to determine the points of intersection algebraically.

Overall Expectations

A2. Determine the zeros and the maximum or minimum of a quadratic function, and solve problems involving quadratic functions, including problems arising from real-world applications.

Specific Expectations

A2.5 solve problems involving the intersection of a linear function and a quadratic function graphically and algebraically (e.g., determine the time when two identical cylindrical water tanks contain equal volumes of water, if one tank is being filled at a constant rate and the other is being emptied through a hole in the bottom).

Learning Goals

  • Students will solve problems involving the intersection of a linear and a quadratic function.
