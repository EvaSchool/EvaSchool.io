Quadratic Function Models: Solving Quadratic Equations

Lesson Description

I can solve quadratic equations by graphing, factoring, and using the quadratic formula.

Overall Expectations

A2. Determine the zeros and the maximum or minimum of a quadratic function, and solve problems involving quadratic functions, including problems arising from real-world applications.

Specific Expectations

A2.3 Solve problems involving quadratic functions arising from real-world applications and represented using function notation.

Learning Goals

  • Students will solve quadratic equations in multiple ways.
  • Students will use strategies like factoring, completing the square, and using the quadratic formula to solve quadratics.
