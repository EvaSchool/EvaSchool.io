Welcome to Unit Two: Exponential Functions

Learning Goals

  1. Exponential Growth and Decay: Learn how exponential functions model real-world scenarios like population growth and radioactive decay.
  2. Graphing: Understand the characteristics and shapes of exponential function graphs.
  3. Properties: Explore domains, ranges, and behavior under transformations.
  4. Solving Equations: Develop skills in solving exponential equations.
  5. Real-World Applications: Apply exponential functions to solve practical problems.
  6. Linear vs. Exponential Models: Compare and understand when to use each model.
  7. Logarithms: Learn about logarithms as inverses of exponential functions.
  8. Technology Use: Use graphing tools for better understanding and visualization.
