That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

In this lesson, you will:

  1. Use the formula of simple interest to calculate the future value of an investment.
  2. Determine the principal needed in order to have a certain amount in the future.
  3. Explore the idea of compound interest.
  4. Use the formula for compound interest to solve problems.
  5. Explore the idea of an annuity.
  6. Calculate the future value of an annuity.
  7. Use spreadsheet software to analyze annuities.
  8. Change parameters of annuities to offer quick analysis.

Success Criteria

Students will be successful if:

  1. Calculate simple interest.
  2. Calculate compound interest.
  3. Calculate the future and present value of an annuity.
  4. Use spreadsheet software to analyze annuities.
  5. Change parameters of annuities to offer quick analysis.
