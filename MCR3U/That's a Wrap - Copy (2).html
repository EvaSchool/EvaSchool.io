That's a Wrap!

Learning Goals

Students will be able to:

  1. Explore relations that appear to repeat at regular intervals.
  2. Graph relations that appear to repeat at regular intervals.
  3. Graph the parent sine and cosine functions.
  4. Determine the amplitude and period of the parent sine and cosine functions.
  5. Identify the domain and range of sinusoidal functions.
  6. Use the amplitude and period of functions to determine characteristics of their graph.
  7. Define transformations of sine and cosine functions.
  8. Use transformations to graph sine and cosine functions.
  9. Analyze the graphs and tables of values of sinusoidal functions.
  10. Write the equations for sinusoidal functions.

Success Criteria

Students can..

  1. Interpret and describe graphs that repeat at regular intervals.
  2. Examine the two functions that are associated with all sinusoidal functions.
  3. Relate details of sinusoidal phenomena to their graphs.
  4. Identify the key properties of sinusoidal functions.
  5. Use transformations to graph sine and cosine functions.
  6. Solve problems related to real-world applications of sinusoidal functions.
