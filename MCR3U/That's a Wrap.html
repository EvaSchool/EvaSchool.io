That's a Wrap!

Please review the following Learning Goals of this section and check the Success Criteria you already achieved.

Learning Goals

Students can add and subtract binomial and trinomial expressions.

Students can simplify polynomial expressions.

Students can multiply and factor polynomial expressions.

Students can simplify rational expressions by factoring their numerators and denominators and Identify restrictions on rational expressions.

Success Criteria

You will know that you are successful if you can:

  • Simplify polynomials after adding them
  • Subtract polynomials
  • Multiply polynomial expressions with each other.
  • Factor polynomial expressions using various methods.
  • Multiply rational expressions.
  • Divide rational expressions.
  • Simplify rational expressions through factoring.
  • Add rational expressions.
  • Subtract rational expressions.
  • Use common denominators to simplify rational expressions
