Communication, Media, and the Communication Process

Lesson Description

In this lesson, you will learn about the different types of communication and media. You will also learn more about the communication process. You will answer the worksheet provided as an assignment.

Overall Expectations

  • D1  Understanding Media Texts: demonstrate an understanding of a variety of media texts.
  • D2 Understanding Media Forms, Conventions, and Techniques: identify some media forms and explain how the conventions and techniques associated with them are used to create meaning.

Specific Expectations

  • D1.1 Explain how media texts, including complex and challenging texts, are created to suit particular purposes and audiences.
  • D1.2 Interpret media texts, including complex or challenging texts, identifying and explaining with increasing insight the overt and implied messages they convey.
  • D1.4 Explain, with increasing insight, why the same media text might prompt different responses from different audiences.
  • D2.1 Identify general and specific characteristics of a variety of media forms and demonstrate insight into the way they shape content and create meaning.

Learning Goals

  • Students will explain communication, the communication process, and media.
  • Students will identify the communication process in day-to-day activities.
  • Students will interpret messages transmitted through different media channels.

Success Criteria

You will know you are successful in this lesson if you:

  • Explain communication, the communication process, and media.
  • Identify the communication process in day-to-day activities.
  • Interpret messages transmitted through different media channels.
