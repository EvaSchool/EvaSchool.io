Children of Blood and Bone Intro

 

Enter Zélie “Zél” Adebola, the headstrong protagonist of Tomi Adeyemi’s debut fantasy novel, Children of Blood and Bone.

 

 

The first of three books, Children of Blood and Bone, follows the adventures of Zél in her quest to bring back magic to the people of Orïsha.

The story takes place in the fictional kingdom of Orïsha after the ruthless King Saran ordered the slaughter of Majis, people who possess magic, leaving citizens impoverished and oppressed by the ruling class.
