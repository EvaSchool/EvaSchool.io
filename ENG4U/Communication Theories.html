THE BOBO DOLL EXPERIMENT 

This is an experiment conducted by Albert Bandura in which he proves that children also learn by observing what adults are doing and doing it themselves.

This experiment paved the way for the social learning theory.

 

SOCIAL LEARNING THEORY

  • This means that people learn from observing other people’s actions.
  • Applying this to media means that people adapt and follow the behavior they view in media.
