Lesson 4.5: Media Production: Producing Videos - Content

ALL SCRIPTS MUST HAVE: 

  • Script Title
  • Director/Writer’s Names
  • Treatment: the script’s outline in paragraph form
  • (Optional) Scene Numbers
  • (Optional) Duration
  • An Audio column: Contains the lines, sound effects, and music of the script
  • A Visual column: Contains the shots and (sometimes) storyboard of the script
